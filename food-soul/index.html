Home Cooking for the Soul

Located inside City Kitch on Thrift Road in Charlotte's West End, Food For Your Soul CLT is famous for inventing the **Cornbread Bowl**. We take a freshly baked, honey-glazed cornbread cake, hollow it into a bowl, and load it with slow-cooked collards, sweet candied yams, four-cheese mac, and gourmet meats.
