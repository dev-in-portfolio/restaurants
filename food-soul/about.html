Our Southern Soul Roots

Charlotte's viral cornbread bowl innovators, cooking with comfort seasonings out of the West End.

Operating Inside City Kitch

Food For Your Soul CLT operates inside the state-of-the-art culinary facility, City Kitch, located at 2200 Thrift Road in Charlotte. By focusing on a cloud-kitchen format, we direct all our attention to cooking premium, high-volume takeout batches. We do not offer table service, but grab-and-go ordering from our lobby counters.

Every order is packaged hot in insulated containers to ensure your soul food remains steaming until you reach home.

Invention of the Cornbread Bowl

The cornbread bowl was created as a delicious solution to a simple problem: how to enjoy all your favorite Southern sides and meats together. Our founders hollowed out a sweet honeycomb corn cake, filling the basin with baked mac & cheese, sweet candied yams, and turkey collard greens.

What started as a family kitchen experiment quickly became a viral social media phenomenon across Charlotte, bringing foodies from all over North Carolina to Thrift Road.

Seasoned to Perfection

We believe soul food requires time and care. We import stone-ground cornmeal for our bowls, bake our four-cheese mac & cheese with real cream, slow-steam our collard greens with smoked turkey wings for savory depth, and roast our sweet candied yams under brown sugar and cinnamon blankets.
