About Us

Healthy Food for a Happy World

Our Mission

Healthy Food for a Happy World

We started Flower Child with a simple belief: healthy food should make you feel good — not just physically, but emotionally. Every dish we serve is a celebration of fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and the joy of eating well.

That's why everything we make is crafted from scratch. Our dressings, our sauces, our desserts — every last bite is made fresh daily with real ingredients you can feel good about.

Fresh Ingredients

Made From Scratch

We use organic produce guided by the Environmental Working Group's Dirty Dozen list — prioritizing organic for the items that matter most. Our ingredients are sourced carefully, and everything is prepared in-house with care.

No artificial flavors. No preservatives. No shortcuts. Just real food, made right.
