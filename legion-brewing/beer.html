Our Beers

From flagship favorites to Trolley Barn experiments, every pint reflects our commitment to craft.

Flagship IPA

Juicy Jay IPA

7.2% ABV

Our crown jewel. A hazy New England-style IPA loaded with Citra, Mosaic, and Galaxy hops. Explosive notes of orange, mango, and passion fruit with a velvety-smooth finish.

Classic Stout

Penguin Stout

5.8% ABV

A Charlotte institution. Rich roasted barley and chocolate malt deliver deep notes of cocoa, espresso, and a whisper of vanilla. Silky, sessionable, unforgettable.

Seasonal

Sunshine Kölsch

4.8% ABV

Warm-weather crusher. Delicately hopped with German Tradition, crackery pilsner malt, and a crisp, clean finish. Available spring through summer.

Seasonal

Autumn Ember Amber

5.6% ABV

A toasted amber ale with caramel malt backbone, subtle roast, and a hint of cinnamon. Fireside drinking at its finest.

Seasonal

Winter Solstice Barleywine

11.2% ABV

Bourbon barrel-aged barleywine with massive notes of toffee, dark fruit, and oak. Cellar-worthy and warming.

Trolley Barn Exclusive

Foeder-Soured Golden

6.4% ABV

Aged in oak foeders for 12 months with wild Brettanomyces. Bright acidity, stone fruit, and a funky, complex finish. Only at Trolley Barn.
