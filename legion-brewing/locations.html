About This Location

Our smallest and most experimental outpost. The Trolley Barn is dedicated to pushing boundaries — foeder-aged sours, spontaneous fermentation, and one-off collaborations you'll only find here.

Note: No full kitchen. Limited food menu of snacks, cheese boards, and rotating pop-ups.

Our largest location houses the 15-barrel production brewery and Charlotte's most inviting beer garden. The brick-oven pizza program — led by Chef David Park — has become a destination in its own right.

Highlight: 40-tap indoor bar + two outdoor bars in the biergarten. Live music most weekends.
