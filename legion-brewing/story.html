Our Story

What started as a vision in Plaza Midwood has grown into four Charlotte destinations united by craft, community, and a little bit of rebellion.

How We Began

In 2015, a group of friends with a shared love of great beer and bold food took a chance on a forgotten music store in Plaza Midwood. The former Brodt's Music building — a Charlotte landmark since the 1950s — sat empty until we saw its potential. We kept the bones, rewired the soul, and built Charlotte's first true brewpub.

2015

Legion Brewing opens at 1906 Commonwealth Ave in the former Brodt's Music building. A 7-barrel brewhouse, a scratch kitchen, and the first pints of what would become Juicy Jay IPA.

2018

SouthPark location debuts at 5610 Carnegie Blvd. A new chapter with the Capitol Room and Tower Room — Charlotte's most distinctive private event spaces.

2021

Trolley Barn opens at 2104 South Blvd. A dedicated experimental facility serving small-batch, barrel-aged, and wild ales exclusive to this location.

2023

West Morehead production facility and biergarten launches at 2013 W Morehead St. A 15-barrel system, brick-oven pizza program, and Charlotte's most welcoming outdoor beer garden.

What We Stand For
