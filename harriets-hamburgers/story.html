Our Mantra

Just Be Good

That's the mantra. Simple, direct, and it means everything.

We believe sharing food is one of the most basic acts of kindness. And that's what we're all about at Harriet's — happiness through hamburgers. Every decision we make comes back to that simple idea: use better ingredients, treat people well, and make the world a little brighter one burger at a time.

"Quality ingredients, no compromises. Hamburgers up hot and fast, with all-natural angus beef, premium cheese, all-natural sodas, and always with a side of kindness."

— The Harriet's Way
