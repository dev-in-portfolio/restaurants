Welcome to Harriet's

Voted Charlotte's Best Hamburger by the Charlotte Observer

At Harriet's our formula is simple: quality ingredients, no compromises. We serve hamburgers up hot and fast, with all-natural angus beef, premium cheese, all-natural sodas and always with a side of kindness. We're committed to cleaner food — no antibiotics, no hormones, no high-fructose corn syrup, and we're transitioning to 100% beef tallow frying.
