Sourcing & Heritage

Our journey of bringing authentic sweet, sour, salty, and spicy flavors to Charlotte's Ballantyne community.

From Bangkok to Ballantyne

At Bahn Thai, we believe that authentic food starts with family recipes. Our chefs bring decades of experience from kitchens in Bangkok to Charlotte, NC, crafting dishes that honor the rich regional variations of Thai comfort food.

Nestled in the heart of Ballantyne on Copper Way, our dining space offers a warm, wood-accented atmosphere perfect for couples, family dinners, and business lunches.

Lemongrass, Galangal & Lime Leaves

To capture the true depth of Thai curries, we import fresh herbs and spices weekly. Fresh kaffir lime leaves, aromatic galangal root, lemongrass stalks, and sweet Thai basil are hand-crushed in our kitchen to create our signature green and red curry bases.

By grinding our own spices and slow-cooking our broth in small batches, we ensure every bowl has the complex, layered flavor profile traditional Thai cuisine is celebrated for.

The Art of Thai & Japanese Sushi

In addition to our traditional wok entrees, Bahn Thai houses a full-service sushi bar. Our experienced sushi chefs select premium fish daily to prepare classic sashimi, nigiri, and creative specialty rolls like the Ballantyne Fire Roll. We strive to provide a dining experience where classic Thai wok heat meets fresh Japanese precision.
