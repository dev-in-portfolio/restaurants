Our Story

A family tradition of Vietnamese hospitality on South Boulevard

Doan's Vietnamese Cuisine began with a simple vision: to share the vibrant, aromatic flavors of Vietnam with Charlotte. Founded in 2009 by the Doan family, the restaurant started as a small storefront on South Boulevard with modest seating and an even more modest kitchen — but the family's recipes were anything but modest.

Drawing on generations of Vietnamese cooking traditions, the Doan family built a menu around the dishes they grew up eating: pho simmered for hours with bones and spices, spring rolls wrapped fresh to order, and broken rice plates that transported diners straight to the streets of Saigon.

Over the years, Doan's has grown from a hidden gem to a South Charlotte staple. Regulars return week after week for the same bowls of pho, and newcomers quickly discover why this family-run kitchen has earned its reputation as one of the most authentic Vietnamese restaurants in the city.

Today, we continue to cook the way we always have — from scratch, with fresh ingredients, and without MSG. Every bowl of pho, every spring roll, and every plate of broken rice is made with the same care and pride that the Doan family brought to Charlotte more than 15 years ago.

Our Journey

2009
A New Beginning
The Doan family opens Doan's Vietnamese Cuisine on South Boulevard, serving family recipes from a small kitchen with eight tables.
2012
Growing Reputation
Word spreads about the pho and broken rice. Regulars begin returning weekly, and the restaurant earns a loyal following.
2016
Expanding the Menu
The kitchen expands to offer a wider range of Vietnamese classics — vermicelli bowls, grilled platters, and an extended vegetarian section.
2020
Weathering Together
Through the challenges of the pandemic, the Doan family pivots to takeout and delivery, keeping the community fed and the kitchen running.
Today
Still Cooking with Love
Doan's remains a family operation — still no MSG, still made from scratch, still welcoming everyone like family.

What We Believe

Family Roots

This is a family restaurant in every sense. The recipes come from our family, and we treat every guest like extended family.

Authenticity

We don't cut corners or chase trends. Our food reflects the real flavors of Vietnam — bold, fresh, and honest.

Community

South Charlotte is our home. We're proud to serve our neighbors and be part of the vibrant dining scene on South Boulevard.
