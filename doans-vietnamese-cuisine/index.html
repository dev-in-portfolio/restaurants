Family-Owned Since 2009

Doan's Vietnamese Cuisine has been serving Charlotte's South Boulevard corridor for over 15 years. What started as a small family operation has grown into a beloved destination for authentic pho, fresh spring rolls, and Vietnamese comfort food made from scratch every day.

We take pride in our no-MSG kitchen, generous portions, and the kind of hospitality that makes every guest feel like family.
