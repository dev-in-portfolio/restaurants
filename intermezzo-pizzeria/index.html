No Freezers. No Microwaves.

At Intermezzo Pizzeria, "fresh" isn't a marketing word — it's a kitchen rule. There are no freezers on site. No microwaves. Every pizza is built on handmade dough stretched to order. The mozzarella is made in-house. The sauces simmer for hours. The vegetables are prepped daily.

This commitment to scratch cooking extends across the entire menu — from Serbian classics like cevapi and stuffed peppers to Italian favorites like veggie lasagna and pasta.
