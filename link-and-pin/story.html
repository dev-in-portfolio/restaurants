Our Story

Named after the coupling that connects railcars — Link & Pin is about bringing people together over exceptional food and drink.

Why "Link & Pin"?

Before modern couplers, railroad cars were connected using a "link" and a "pin." A link was installed on one railcar, and when two railcars were joined, a pin was manually inserted into the other car's link — binding them together.

Our flagship South End location sits on the light rail line, and the name felt like a natural fit. We connect people — over a shared meal, a perfectly mixed cocktail, a celebration, or a quiet Tuesday night dinner.

2018

Restaurateur Rob Duckworth announces Link & Pin — a new concept for Historic South End at The Bradham, steps from the light rail.

2019

Link & Pin South End opens at 155 New Bern St to immediate acclaim — guests embrace the warm, industrial interior and chef-driven menu.

2021

Link & Pin Arboretum opens at 8128 Providence Road, bringing the same elevated dining experience to Charlotte's southeast side.

2023

Link & Pin NorthCross opens in Huntersville — our third and newest location, serving the Lake Norman community.

Our culinary-driven menu is at the center of the restaurant experience, with food that's chef-inspired but approachable. Our cozy bars provide an ideal setting for guests to gather and sip on a glass of wine or a delicious cocktail mixed fresh by one of our experienced bartenders.

Each location has its own personality, but they're all connected by the same commitment: warm hospitality, high-quality ingredients, and a space where everyone feels welcome.
