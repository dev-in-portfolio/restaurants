Welcome

Creative American Tapas
on Montford Drive

For over 17 years, Chef Bruce Moffett has been serving up bold, eclectic small plates in the heart of South Charlotte. Named a James Beard semi-finalist for Best New Restaurant in 2010, Good Food on Montford is where adventurous eaters come to share, sip, and discover something new.

Every dish is a conversation starter. Pull up a chair, order a few plates, and let us do the rest.
