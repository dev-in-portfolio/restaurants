Chef's Counter Experience

For the ultimate culinary experience, request seats at the chef's counter. Watch Chef Bruce Moffett and his team work the pass, plate by plate, right in front of you. It's the best seat in the house — interactive, intimate, and unforgettable.

Small Plates, Big Experience

Our menu is built for sharing. Order 3–5 plates per person, start with something cold, work your way through warm and savory, and don't forget dessert. Your server will guide you through the menu and help you build the perfect spread.

What to Expect

Expect a lively, trendy atmosphere with moderate noise. Business casual attire is the norm. We have outdoor seating for when the weather is right, and our full bar means your pre-dinner cocktail is never far away.

Perfect For

Date Nights & Group Gatherings

Whether it's a romantic evening for two or a lively dinner with friends, Good Food on Montford is the place to gather, share, and celebrate over creative small plates.
