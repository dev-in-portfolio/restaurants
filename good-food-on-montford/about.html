Our Story

About Us

Chef Bruce Moffett

Creative American Tapas on Montford Drive

Good Food on Montford is Chef Bruce Moffett's sophomore effort — a bold, eclectic small-plates restaurant that has become one of Charlotte's most beloved dining destinations. Located on the vibrant Montford Drive corridor in South Charlotte, the restaurant has been a fixture of the local food scene for over 17 years.

In 2010, Chef Moffett was named a James Beard semi-finalist for Best New Restaurant, an honor that put Good Food on Montford on the national culinary map. But for regulars, the recognition was no surprise — they'd been coming back for the creative, shareable plates and the warm, convivial atmosphere since day one.

The Concept

Small plates meant for sharing. Order a few dishes, pass them around, and discover new flavor combinations together. Every plate is designed to spark conversation and curiosity — from Southern Farmhouse Cheeses with truffled chutney to Korean Beef with crispy rice and scallion kimchi.

17 Years of Good Food

For nearly two decades, we've been proving that great food doesn't have to be fussy. Our menu is creative without being pretentious, seasonal without being predictable. We cook with intention, source with care, and serve with a smile.

Eclectic Wine List & Full Bar

Our wine list is intentionally eclectic — a curated selection of bottles from around the world, chosen to complement our bold, layered flavors. Add a full bar with craft cocktails and you've got the perfect pairing for any plate.

The Chef's Counter

Pull up a seat at the chef's counter and watch the kitchen team in action. It's the best seat in the house — a front-row view of the creativity, precision, and energy that goes into every dish. It's dining as entertainment.
