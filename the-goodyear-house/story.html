Our Story

A restored mill home, a neighborhood, a philosophy of growth

Why "Goodyear"?

A tradition of adding on when times are good

In the early 1900s, when a family had a prosperous year, they would add a room, a porch, or a second story to their home. These additions — sometimes mismatched in color or style — told the story of growth and resilience. When we found this 1900s mill house in NoDa, we knew it deserved to carry that tradition forward. The Goodyear House is a place that grows with its community, honoring the past while embracing the future.

1900s
Original mill home built in the NoDa neighborhood of Charlotte, NC
2010s
NoDa transforms into Charlotte's Arts & Entertainment District — vibrant, creative, growing
2020
Built On Hospitality finds the mill home and begins restoration
2021
The Goodyear House opens its doors — a neighborhood restaurant in a historic home
Today
A beloved gathering place for Charlotte — serving dinner, lunch, weekend brunch, and hosting private events, weddings, and celebrations in our garden and greenhouse
