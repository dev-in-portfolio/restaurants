What is a Goodyear House?

In the early 1900s, when folks had a good year, they would add on to their homes.

The house changed shape when times were good. In leaner years, it stood still. Each addition — sometimes mismatched in color or style — marked a chapter of growth. When The Goodyear House came to life in this 1900s mill home, we embraced that story. The design blends historic charm with modern updates. Just like NoDa and Charlotte, we're still growing. And we've saved you a seat. Welcome home.

Built on Hospitality

The Goodyear House is the first restaurant by Built On Hospitality, a Charlotte-based group passionate about crafting exceptional culinary experiences that resonate with guests and leave an indelible mark on their memories.

Executive Chef Chris Coleman — a Food Network Champion with appearances on Chopped, Beat Bobby Flay, and Alex vs. America — leads a kitchen rooted in Southern flavors and creativity.
