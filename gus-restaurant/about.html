Greek-American Comfort Cooking

Gus' Family Restaurant was opened in 1998 by Gus and his family in Monroe, NC. Combining Southern American comfort cooking with our family's Mediterranean heritage, we focus on serving generous portions of homemade Salisbury steaks, crispy fried pickle chips, and loaded Greek salads.

Everything we serve is cooked fresh to order, importing olive oil and mountain oregano directly from our family olive groves in Greece to guarantee authentic Greek seasonings.

Walkup Avenue Landmark

Over the past 28 years, our dining rooms on Walkup Avenue have served as a cozy gathering hub for Monroe families. Grandparents who dined with us in the late 90s now bring their grandkids to enjoy warm flans, baked spaghettis, and gyro plates. We thank you for letting us feed your family.
