The Culinary Artisan

Classical French refinement meets the vibrant ingredients of the Carolinas.

Chef Adam Reed

At the core of Santé Fine Dining is Chef Adam Reed. Having trained under master chefs in French gastronomy, Chef Reed opened Santé in 2002 with a dream to bring classical French culinary techniques to Matthews. His vision is defined by meticulous preparation, elegant presentation, and clean, layered flavors that celebrate the art of dining.

Chef Reed can be found in the kitchen daily, overseeing the preparation of stocks, slow-reducing sauces, and tailoring menus to match the changing seasons.

Sourcing Philosophy

While our inspiration is classic French, our ingredients are proudly local. Santé works in partnership with family farms in Union County and across the Carolinas to secure organic herbs, heirloom vegetables, and pasture-raised meats.

We believe fine dining is a reflection of the earth. By changing our menus seasonally, we ensure that every plate captures the Peak of freshness and local terroir.

Matthews Town Green

Santé is housed in a beautifully preserved historic building overlooking the tree-lined Matthews Town Green. Built with classic brickwork and expansive windows, our location provides a romantic, European-streetscape view. Over the past two decades, we have grown alongside Matthews, serving as a landmark destination where locals and visitors gather to celebrate life's most precious moments.
