An Elegant Culinary Escape

Led by Chef Adam Reed, Santé Fine Dining offers a sophisticated dining experience that honors classical French cooking techniques while embracing local Carolinas sourcing. Located in the heart of historic Matthews, our intimate, warm-lit dining rooms provide the perfect setting for anniversaries, wine pairings, and quiet celebrations.
