Carte du Dîner

A seasonal collection of French-influenced fine dining dishes, crafted by Chef Adam Reed using the freshest local Carolinas ingredients.

Hors d'œuvres

Escargots HelixSignature $17.00

Plump Burgundy snails slow-baked in garlic-herb butter, accompanied by sautéed gourmet mushrooms and a flaky puff pastry crown.

Soupe à l'Oignon Classique $12.00

Caramelized sweet onions slow-cooked in a rich, deeply flavored beef bone broth, topped with toasted French baguette and a thick broiled Gruyère cheese crust.

Jumbo Lump Crab Cakes $18.00

Pan-seared jumbo lump crab cakes seasoned with herbs, served over a bed of baby arugula and a refreshing lemon-basil remoulade.

Plats Principaux

Filet Mignon Burgundy $49.00

Grilled Certified Angus Beef Filet Mignon accompanied by oven-roasted fingerling potatoes, baby root vegetables, and a rich, velvety burgundy reduction.

Seared Duck Breast $42.00

Crispy pan-seared duck breast served over a savory rosemary-infused polenta cake, buttered haricots verts, and a sweet-tart strawberry balsamic reduction.

Atlantic Roasted Salmon $38.00

Pan-roasted Atlantic salmon served over a light lemon-herb couscous, buttered asparagus spears, and finished with a citrus beurre blanc.

Desserts

Grand Marnier Crêpes $12.00

Two delicate French crêpes flambéed in orange-infused Grand Marnier liqueur and sweet butter syrup, served with vanilla bean ice cream.

Classic Crème Brûlée $11.00

A rich, velvety vanilla bean custard base under a caramelized, hand-shattered burnt sugar crust. Garnished with fresh seasonal berries.
