Our Story

From a dive bar on the water to Lake Norman's most charming dining destination

A Love Letter to Lake Norman

Hello, Sailor is the second act from husband-and-wife team Joe and Katy Kindred, the acclaimed duo behind Kindred in Davidson. After putting Davidson on the culinary map with their flagship restaurant — named one of America's best new restaurants by Bon Appetit — the Kindreds set their sights on the water.

"We want people to be able to park their boat and grab a quick meal or come with friends and family and linger and share," says Joe. "The menu reflects that balance."

The couple was initially looking in Charlotte but couldn't resist a lakeside restaurant with its own dock. The building — roughly 4,000 square feet with a dining room, bar, three-tiered patio, and a seasonal tiki bar — was the former Rusty Rudder, a beloved (if slightly divey) Lake Norman institution.

"North Carolina fish camp meets mid-century Palm Springs."

— Joe Kindred, on the Hello, Sailor concept

The transformation was dramatic. Gone are the dark, worn-in corners of the Rudder. In their place: bright, open spaces with leather slingback chairs, white glass globe lighting, chartreuse accents, and wicker-backed bar stools. The vibe is equal parts Brady Bunch and beach club — retro without being kitschy, refined without being stuffy.

Today, Hello, Sailor is recognized by the Michelin Guide and beloved by Lake Norman locals and visitors alike. Dogs are welcome on the patio. Boat shoes are encouraged. And the sunsets? Unforgettable.

The Timeline

Kindred Opens in Davidson

2015

Joe and Katy Kindred open Kindred in Davidson, quickly earning a reputation as one of North Carolina's most exciting restaurants.

Bon Appetit Best New Restaurant

2017

Kindred is named one of America's Best New Restaurants by Bon Appetit magazine, cementing the Kindreds' place in the culinary landscape.

Hello, Sailor Opens

2021

Hello, Sailor debuts at the former Rusty Rudder location on Lake Norman. Retro-chic design, chef-driven seafood, and 20 boat slips.

Michelin Guide Recognition

2024

Hello, Sailor is recognized by the MICHELIN Guide, selected for its exceptional contemporary cuisine and lakeside setting.
