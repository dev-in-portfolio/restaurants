Welcome to Hello, Sailor

North Carolina fish camp meets mid-century Palm Springs — right on the shores of Lake Norman.

Waterfront Dining

Three-tiered patio with sweeping lake views, a seasonal tiki bar, and 20 boat slips for diners arriving by water.

🐟

Chef-Driven Seafood

Fresh PEI oysters, Calabash shrimp, smoked fish dip, and creative coastal fare from the Kindred culinary team.

Tiki Bar & Cocktails

Seasonal outdoor tiki bar with tropical cocktails, frozen drinks, and a carefully curated wine and beer list.

Lake Norman's Premier Waterfront Spot

Perched on the shores of Lake Norman at Holiday Marina, Hello, Sailor offers an experience that's equal parts relaxed and refined. Inside, find mid-century charm with a wood-burning stove. Outside, soak up the sun on our multi-level deck.
