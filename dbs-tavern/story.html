Our Story

Locally owned, family-run since 2014

DB's Tavern was founded in 2014 by Don Burgoon and his son Don Jr. — a father-son team with deep roots in Charlotte's restaurant scene. Already known for running Picasso's Sports Cafe, the Burgoons saw a need for a welcoming neighborhood tavern in the growing Ballantyne area and decided to build one from the ground up.

The concept was simple: great food, cold beer, and a place where everyone knows your name. DB's quickly became exactly that — a go-to spot for families, sports fans, and neighbors looking for a reliable meal and a friendly face. The menu was built around the things people actually want: hand-rolled pizza rolls, award-winning wings, hand-pressed burgers, and ice-cold drafts.

Over the years, DB's has become a Ballantyne institution. The walls are lined with TVs for game day, the kitchen stays open late, and the staff treats every guest like a regular. Whether you're coming in for lunch after a soccer game or closing the place down after a late shift, DB's is always ready for you.
