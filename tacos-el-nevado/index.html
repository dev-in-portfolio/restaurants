The Art of the Street Taco

Operating on Central Ave and South Blvd in Charlotte, Tacos El Nevado is dedicated to serving authentic Mexican street food with a special focus on Oaxacan seasonings. Every day, our kitchen grinds corn to hand-press fresh tortillas, slow-braises our meats, and roasts fresh chilies for our iconic Oaxacan salsa boards.
