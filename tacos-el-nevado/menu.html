Mexican & Oaxacan Board

Antojitos & Tacos

Street TacosHand-Pressed Masa $3.00 /ea

Served on double hand-pressed fresh corn tortillas with chopped cilantro, onions, lime, and radish wedges. Choose Al Pastor, Carnitas, Carne Asada, Barbacoa, or Lengua (+$0.50).

Quesabirria Combo $13.00

Three griddled corn tortillas folded with melted stringy cheese, shredded beef birria, onions, and cilantro. Served with a cup of hot consomé dipping broth.

Sopes $4.50 /ea

Thick corn masa boats with pinched edges, layered with refried black beans, choice of meat, lettuce, crumbled cotija cheese, and Mexican crema.

Tortas, stews & Aguas

Torta Oaxaqueña $10.50

Toasted telera sandwich bread stuffed with breaded steak (milanesa), black beans, avocado slices, stringy quesillo cheese, pickled jalapeños, and tomatoes.

Oaxacan Clay-Pot Tlayuda $14.50

Large, crispy toasted corn tortilla spread with pork lard (asiento), black bean purée, shredded quesillo cheese, cabbage, and tasajo (thinly sliced grilled beef).

Horchata & Jamaica $3.50

Traditional ice-cold sweet rice milk flavored with cinnamon sticks and vanilla, or sweet hibiscus flower tea (Jamaica) poured fresh.
