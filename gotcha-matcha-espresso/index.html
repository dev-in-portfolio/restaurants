Our Story

From Barista Dream to
Charlotte's #1 Matcha

Eric "Braek Haven" Hicklen Jr. was working as a Starbucks barista in Columbia, SC when a 2019 trip to Thailand introduced him to matcha served in a Belgian waffle cone. That moment sparked a vision.

He launched a pop-up in September 2023, moved through co-working spaces, and by November 2024 opened a permanent Uptown location. By March 2026 — two Uptown spots and a Yelp/Charlotte Observer award as Charlotte's top matcha spot.
