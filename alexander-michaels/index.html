The Fourth Ward Sanctuary

For over forty years, Alexander Michael's—affectionately known by locals as "Al Mike's"—has stood as a cozy neighborhood haven in Uptown Charlotte's historic Fourth Ward. Housed in a beautifully preserved brick building dating back to 1897, our tavern offers a warm retreat with antique wood paneling, dark green leather booths, and hearty pub classics.
