Feed your home tailgate, office meeting, or neighborhood gathering with Al Mike's signature comfort pasta trays and slider boxes.

Game Day Slider Boxes

Elevate your tailgate. We offer boxed trays of 12 or 24 mini Al Mike's pub cheeseburgers and seared turkey sliders, wrapped hot in foil and served with crisp crinkle-cut fries.

Bulk Pasta Trays

Feed the crowd. Large oven-ready pans of our famous "What It Is" Cajun chicken pasta or sweet-savory Honey Chicken Pasta. Serves 10 to 15 guests, paired with warm French bread.

Spicy Crab Soup Pots

Serve our most requested starter. Quarts of our rich, creamy Spicy Crab Soup, complete with individual serving bowls, oyster crackers, and hot sauce.
