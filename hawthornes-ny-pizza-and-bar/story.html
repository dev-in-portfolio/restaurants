Our Story

Sharing pizza, building community — since 2004

How It All Began

A simple idea: bring true New York pizza to Charlotte

Sharing a meal is powerful. It brings people together, shapes our memories, and builds community. Hawthorne's NY Pizza & Bar was established in 2004 on 7th Street in Charlotte with a simple mission: serve authentic New York-style pizza in a welcoming neighborhood atmosphere. From day one, the focus was on quality — fresh dough made daily, premium cheese, the finest toppings, and a foldable thin crust that rivals anything north of the Mason-Dixon.

2004
Hawthorne's opens on 7th Street in Charlotte — serving true NY-style pizza to Charlotteans
2010s
Growth across Charlotte metro — new locations in Rea Road, Carmel Road, Huntersville, and beyond
2020s
Expansion to 10 locations including South End, Concord, Mooresville, and Mint Hill — each with full bar and patio seating
Today
A Charlotte institution — serving pizza, wings, pasta, and craft beer at 10 neighborhood locations across the metro
