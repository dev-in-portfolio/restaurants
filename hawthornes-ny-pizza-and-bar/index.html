Welcome to Hawthorne's

True New York tasting pizza — right here in Charlotte

Sharing a meal is powerful. It brings people together, shapes our memories, and builds community. Hawthorne's NY Pizza & Bar was established in 2004 to do just that. With true New York-style pizza, fresh ingredients, and a neighborhood atmosphere, we've grown to 10 locations across the Charlotte metro — each one a gathering place for friends, families, and pizza lovers.
