Frequently Asked

Do I need a reservation?

Yes. Reservations are required. We seat only 16 guests per evening, and our tasting menus sell out quickly. Book through Tock to secure your seat.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 2–4 weeks in advance. Popular dates and weekends fill even faster. Join our Tock notify list to be alerted when new dates open.

Is there a dress code?

We ask that guests dress smart casual. No strict dress code, but we encourage you to dress for the occasion.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes. Please note any dietary restrictions or allergies when booking through Tock, and our team will accommodate you. Certain seasonal themes may not be suitable for children under 18 — contact us in advance.

What is the cancellation policy?

As a prepaid reservation, cancellations and modifications must be made through Tock. Please review the terms at the time of booking.

Is parking available?

Yes. We have on-street and lot parking available in the Wesley Heights area.
