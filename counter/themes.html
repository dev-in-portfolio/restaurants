Philosophy of Sensory Pairing

Acoustic Waveforms

We match the frequency and pitch of the room's soundtrack to the primary tasting profiles of each dish. High pitch enhances sweetness, while bass notes accentuate umami and salt.

Chroma Projection

Overhead projection maps custom animations onto the dinner plates. The colors of the projection (e.g. violet, warm amber, emerald green) are calibrated to visually support tasting notes.

Olfactory Diffusers

Our custom ventilation system releases subtle scent profiles (forest pine, burnt sugarcane, ozone breeze) to match course backdrops without overpowering the kitchen's aromas.
