Our Philosophy

−

counter-balance

Designed to showcase the produce and proteins from the land around us, rather than perpetuate the unstable alternative. We source from over 20 local farms and maintain a fermentation and preservation program to ensure nothing goes to waste.

−

counter-intuitive

Contrary to the common-sense expectation of service, but nevertheless true. Submerging our guests with visuals, sounds, and feelings along with taste. A theatrical performance at the counter.

−

counter-culture

Opposed to the norms of the restaurant industry, promoting diversity and unveiling narratives that have been hidden. We believe hospitality should be equitable, sustainable, and deeply human.
