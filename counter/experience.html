The Experience

A Theater of Taste

Similar to a theatrical performance, counter- is a shared experience. Each evening is a fully immersive journey through sound, taste, and art.

counter- is a Michelin starred tasting menu concept by Chef Sam Hart. We are a story-driven dining experience focused on producing the highest level personal memory for guests, while showcasing our region in a singular composition.

The Evening

Once seated at the U-shaped counter surrounding the kitchen, guests experience meticulously coursed-out dishes presented by Chef Hart and his team. Each course arrives with its own story, its own soundtrack, its own visual language. The evening unfolds like chapters in a book you never want to end.

The Menu

Our tasting menu rotates quarterly, driven by sense-based storylines drawn from music, modern art, mental health, and cultural moments. Recent themes have explored Frida Kahlo, Andy Warhol, and the works of Roald Dahl. Each theme is a complete world — from the dishes to the tableware to the art on the walls.

The Beverage Program

Wine and spirit-free beverage pairings are available with every tasting menu. Our wine program features a diverse offering that includes female, LGBTQ+, and minority winemakers — alongside a thoughtfully curated non-alcoholic pairing option.
