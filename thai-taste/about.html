Legacy of the Wok

Matthews Station's favorite dining landmark, bringing Charlotte's original Thai recipes to life since 2005.

Established in Matthews 2005

Thai Taste Matthews was established in 2005 at Matthews Station Street, expanding the legacy of Charlotte's original pioneering Thai restaurant. As a family-owned branch of the restaurant that first introduced Thai flavors to the Queen City, we have spent over two decades preserving traditional wok cooking styles in historic Matthews.

Conveniently located directly across from Matthews Town Hall and Library, we serve as a bustling, favorite gathering place for downtown workers and families alike.

lemongrass, Galangal & Sweet Basil

Authentic Thai flavor relies entirely on the quality of its fresh herbs. We source imported kaffir lime leaves, fresh galangal roots, lemongrass stalks, sweet Thai basil, and fiery bird's eye chilies to hand-craft our curry pastes and broth bases from scratch daily.

Whether wok-stirring wide flat noodles over high flame or simmering coconut milk duck curry, our kitchen ensures that every dish honors our heritage.

Matthews Station Landmark

For over 20 years, we have remained committed to providing generous portions, customized heat levels to accommodate every palate, and warm, fast hospitality. We invite you to experience the rich flavors of Thailand right in the heart of downtown Matthews.
