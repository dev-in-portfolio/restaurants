A Heritage of Saffron & Spice

Located across from the Matthews Town Hall & Library, Thai Taste Matthews has been crafting fresh, traditional Thai cuisine since 2005. Rooted in Charlotte's first-ever Thai kitchen, we honor time-tested recipes using fresh Thai herbs, lemongrass, coconut milk, and customized spice levels cooked to order.
