Our Story

Rock n Roll Ramen

Pecan-smoked pork belly, creative bowls, and an edgy izakaya vibe — right on the Blue Line in South End, Charlotte.

Not Your Average Ramen Shop

Futo Buta isn't trying to be a quiet, traditional Japanese restaurant. We're a rock n roll ramen bar in the heart of Charlotte's South End — loud, energetic, and unapologetically creative.

We sit right on the Blue Line light rail tracks at 222 E Bland St. Trains roll by while you slurp your tonkotsu. The vibe is South End through and through — industrial, modern, alive.

🍜
🍖

Pecan-Smoked Pork Belly

Every ramen bowl at Futo Buta starts with our signature pecan-smoked pork belly. Low and slow, pecan wood, until it's tender, smoky, and deeply flavorful.

It's the thing people talk about. It's the thing people come back for. It's in the Tonkotsu, the Buta Bowl, the Pork Belly Buns — it's in our DNA.

Creative Bowls, No Rules

Our menu doesn't play by the rules. The Chizu is a cheese ramen — pecorino, whipped egg, shiso pesto, yuzu. It shouldn't work, but it absolutely does.

The Fire and Ice pairs kimchi broth with hot smoked salmon and fresh mint. The Buta Bowl stacks double noodles with three proteins. We make what we want to eat — and apparently, so do you.

🍱
🍺

Izakaya Vibes

We're not just a ramen counter. Futo Buta is an izakaya — a place to hang, share small plates, and drink. Pork Belly Buns with gochujang BBQ. Tori Karaage with chili mayo. Fried Brussels with bonito flakes dancing on top.

Add a cold Japanese beer or a glass of sake, and you've got the Futo Buta experience. Casual, fun, and nothing like a chain restaurant.
