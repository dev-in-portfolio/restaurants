Three Things We Do Right

Pecan-Smoked Pork Belly

Our house specialty. Pecan-smoked low and slow, then nestled into every signature ramen bowl. It's the soul of Futo Buta.

Edgy, loud, unapologetic. We're in South End facing the Blue Line tracks — this isn't your quiet neighborhood sushi bar.
