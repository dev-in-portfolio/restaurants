🚉 Getting Here

  • Blue Line Light Rail — East/West Blvd station is a short walk. The restaurant faces the tracks — you'll see us from the platform.
  • Parking — Parking garage nearby plus street parking along Bland St and surrounding blocks.
  • Rideshare — Drop-off at 222 E Bland St.

ℹ️ Know Before You Go

  • No reservations — First come, first served. Peak hours can mean a short wait.
  • Outdoor seating — When the weather's right, eat outside facing the Blue Line.
  • WiFi — Free WiFi for when your ramen's too good to leave.
  • Beer & wine — Japanese beer, sake, and a curated drink list.
  • Casual dress — Come as you are. This is South End.
