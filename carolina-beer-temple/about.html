📚 Our Story

About Carolina Beer Temple

Born from a love of Belgian beer, built in Matthews

While traveling through Belgium, seeking out some of the finest beers made in the world, Rob and Megan Jacik learned that 96% of all beer consumed in Belgium is served on draft. That revelation became the foundation for Carolina Beer Temple.

The Mission

Carolina Beer Temple was built on a simple idea: bring the Belgian draft experience to North Carolina. In Belgium, beer isn't just a drink — it's a culture, a craft, a way of life. The Temple exists to share that culture with Charlotte and beyond.

The St. Bernardus Partnership

Carolina Beer Temple is the only official St. Bernardus taproom in the Carolinas. That means we get exclusive access to rare vintages, special releases, and a relationship with one of the world's most renowned Belgian breweries. Our private-label ales — St. Ames Blonde and St. Ames Brune — are brewed by St. Bernardus specifically for the Temple.

The Experience

18 rotating taps of Belgian ales, local brews, and rare finds. A curated bottle shop with hundreds of bottles and cans. Weekly tap takeovers featuring North Carolina breweries. Food truck nights. Beer education. Community gatherings.

It's not just a bar — it's a destination for people who care about what they drink.

The Recognition

In 2026, Carolina Beer Temple was named "Best Bar in North Carolina" by Guide to NC. It's a recognition of what Rob and Megan built: a place where great beer meets great community.
