The Roast

Small-batch, house-roasted — from bean to cup

Our Roasting Philosophy

Every batch is a reflection of where it came from

We roast in small batches to ensure every bean reaches its full potential. Our approach is simple: source the highest-quality green coffee from trusted producers, roast with precision, and serve at peak freshness. Whether you're drinking a pour-over single origin or a classic espresso, you're tasting coffee made with intention.

From light roasts that highlight bright, fruity notes to darker roasts with rich chocolate and caramel depth — there's a Giddy Goat roast for every palate. All available by the cup in our cafés or by the bag to brew at home.
