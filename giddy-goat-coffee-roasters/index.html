Welcome to the Herd

House-roasted coffee, homemade empanadas, and a whole lot of character

The Giddy Goat is a Plaza Midwood cornerstone — a coffee roastery and café that brings the best of specialty coffee to the Charlotte community. From small-batch roasts to an array of hand-crafted breakfast items, everything is made in house with locally sourced ingredients. Oh, and we're a zero-waste shop — all drinkware, plates, and utensils are compostable or recyclable.
