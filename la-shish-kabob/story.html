Our Story

Rooted in tradition, serving Charlotte since 2014.

🍴

A Family Tradition

La Shish Kabob was born from a simple belief: that food brings people together. Our family has been sharing Middle-Eastern hospitality around the dinner table for generations, and in 2014 we decided to bring that warmth to Charlotte.

What started as a small storefront in the Eastland area has grown into a beloved neighborhood destination — but our values have never changed. We still marinate our meats by hand, chop our salads fresh every morning, and greet every guest like they're joining us for a family meal.

“The secret is not in the spices — it's in the love we put into every dish.”

Our recipes come from our grandmother's kitchen. The same techniques she used to prepare lamb, lentils, and herbs for our family are the ones we use today. Every dish at La Shish Kabob carries a piece of our heritage.

📍

Our Journey

2014

La Shish Kabob opens its doors on North Sharon Amity Road, serving classic Middle-Eastern fare to the Eastland community.

2016

Our weekend buffet launches — an immediate hit with local families looking for an authentic, all-you-can-enjoy experience.

2018

We expand our catering service, bringing La Shish Kabob to community events, office lunches, and family celebrations across Charlotte.

2020

Facing unprecedented challenges, we pivot to delivery and takeout, connecting with customers through DoorDash, UberEats, and more.

Today

Still family-owned, still cooking with love. La Shish Kabob continues to serve Charlotte with the same recipes, warmth, and dedication as day one.

🤝

Our Community

Charlotte is more than where we do business — it's home. We're proud to serve the Eastland neighborhood and beyond, and we believe that supporting our community is just as important as serving great food.

From sponsoring local youth sports teams to catering mosque and church gatherings, we show up because our community shows up for us. Every customer who walks through our door is part of the La Shish Kabob family.
