A Neighborhood Fixture

Serving Elizabeth since 2007.

Customshop opened in 2007 as a small, chef-driven restaurant on Elizabeth Avenue. In 2022, Chef Andres Kaifer took over, retaining the name, the concept, and every member of the staff. Today it remains what it has always been: a place where the menu changes weekly, the pasta is made by hand, and the neighborhood feels like home.
