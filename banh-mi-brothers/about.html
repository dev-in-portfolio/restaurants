Sourcing & Heritage

The story of two brothers bringing the vibrant flavor of Vietnamese street food to Charlotte.

The Banh Mi Brothers Vision

Banh Mi Brothers consists of two brothers who are excited to share the traditional street foods they grew up eating. Combining sweet soy marinades with fresh herbs, we opened our kitchen near UNC Charlotte to provide a modern, fast, and authentic street-food experience on East W.T. Harris Boulevard.

Every sandwich represents a balance of French bread influence and rich, fresh Vietnamese fillings.

The Perfect Crispy Baguette

A true Banh Mi stands or falls by the quality of its bread. We source our French baguettes from local bakers who prepare them daily with a thin, crispy golden crust and an incredibly light, airy interior.

Every morning, we warm the rolls to crisp up the crust before spreading them with savory pâté and house mayonnaise, ensuring that first crunch is perfect.

Mama Doan's Recipe Legacy

Many of our recipes—including our signature pork spring rolls and lemongrass marinades—have been passed down through generations. Prepared from scratch using fresh ingredients, Mama Doan's cooking standards guide our grill kitchen daily, ensuring comforting, high-quality street food for every visitor.
