Fresh Vietnamese Street Food

Welcome to Banh Mi Brothers in Charlotte, NC. Located right near UNC Charlotte on W.T. Harris Boulevard, we specialize in authentic, vibrant Vietnamese street food. From our traditional pork, beef, and lemongrass chicken banh mi sandwiches to our noodle bowls and custom boba teas, we prepare every dish with scratch-made ingredients and crispy family heritage.
