Welcome to Lupie's

For nearly forty years, Lupie's Cafe has been Charlotte's go-to spot for down-home cooking and comfort. We are famous for our four distinct styles of homemade chili, served over noodles, rice, or inside seared burger buns. Cozy, welcoming, and full of local memories, we welcome you to pull up a chair.

The Four Chilis

Texas Style
Texas Chili

Thick, hearty, and very spicy. Slow-simmered beef with a rich blend of fiery chili peppers, garlic, and cumin. No beans, just bold, authentic flavor.

Cincinnati Style
Cincinnati Chili

A unique Greek-style chili spiced with hints of cinnamon, allspice, and nutmeg. Unbelievably savory and traditionally served over spaghetti.

Southern Style
Southern Mild Chili

Our classic Southern recipe. Mildly spiced ground beef slow-simmered with pinto beans, tomatoes, onions, and sweet spices. A comforting favorite.

Vegetarian
Veggie Chili

Packed with flavor. Slow-cooked with black beans, pinto beans, bell peppers, sweet corn, tomatoes, and organic spices. Perfect for veggie lovers.
