Home-Style Group Catering

Bring Charlotte's favorite chilis, chili mac, and banana pudding to your next office luncheon or family celebration.

Chili Mac Trays

Feed the group with our signature comfort plate. Large oven-ready trays of Cincinnati or Southern style chili baked hot over spaghetti noodles and covered in melted cheddar cheese. Recommended for 10 to 20 guests.

Chili By The Quart

Mix and match your chilis. Order quarts of our Texas (spicy), Cincinnati, Southern, or Veggie chilis. Served with side containers of sour cream, cheddar, diced onions, and tortilla chips.

Banana Pudding Pans

The perfect dessert. Large dessert pans of our home-style banana pudding, layered with vanilla wafers, sweet bananas, and vanilla whipped cream.
