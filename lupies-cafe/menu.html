Signature Chilis

Texas ChiliSpicy

Very spicy, beanless beef chili simmered with fire-roasted peppers, cumin, and garlic. A fiery Lone Star classic.

Cincinnati Chili

A Greek-style beef chili featuring hints of cinnamon, cocoa, and allspice. Traditionally served over a bed of spaghetti.

Southern Style Chili

Mildly spiced ground beef slow-simmered with pinto beans, tomatoes, onions, and sweet chili spices. Pure comfort.
