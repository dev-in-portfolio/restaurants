Allergy Handoff Process

Purple Plate Protocol

All allergen-safe and gluten-free orders are served on dedicated purple square plates. This ensures our service staff can visually identify safe plates during final delivery.

Manager Validation Check

A certified food safety manager personally reviews every allergen ticket, inspects the prep line, and runs the dish to the guest's table.

Ingredient Separation

Separate Fryers & Grills

We utilize dedicated fryers for all gluten-free preparations (like our signature french fries) and separate grill sectors to prevent cross-contamination.

Color-Coded Utensils

All allergen-safe dishes are prepared using purple tongs, pans, and chef knives stored in sealed sanitizing containers.
