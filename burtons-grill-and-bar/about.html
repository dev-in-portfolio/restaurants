Sourcing & Safety

Understand our kitchen's gold-standard allergy safety protocols, wood-grilling heritage, and local sourcing standards.

Gold-Standard Allergen Protocol

At Burtons Grill & Bar, guest safety is our primary mission. Every order carrying an allergy flag is routed directly to a certified kitchen manager or executive chef. We utilize dedicated preparation tables, distinct purple cooking utensils, and sterile pans. These dishes are served on square plates to signal that the meal was prepared with absolute allergy safety controls.

Hickory Wood-Fired Kitchen

Our kitchen revolves around an open hickory wood-fired grill. Grilling over genuine wood blocks produces a distinct charred crust and smoky essence that gas grills simply cannot replicate. This locks intense juices into our center-cut filets, ribeyes, and half-pound gourmet burgers.

Local Partnerships

We partner with local farms, boutique bakeries, and sustainable seafood catchers to source our ingredients. From certified gluten-free burger buns to local red beets, crisp greens, and fresh catches like coastal haddock, we maintain rigorous quality standards across both Charlotte restaurants.
