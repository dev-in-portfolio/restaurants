Our Spaces

La Caja

The Private Room — Seats 24

La Caja — "the box" — is our intimate semi-private dining room tucked beside the main bar. Warm wood, low light, and a dedicated service entrance create the feeling of your own cantina within the restaurant.

  • Capacity: 16–24 guests seated
  • Style: Family-style shared plates, preset menus
  • Ideal for: Client dinners, birthday celebrations, rehearsal dinners, board meetings
  • AV: 65" display with HDMI, Bluetooth audio
  • Access: Direct from bar or private hallway entrance

Full Restaurant Buyout — Up to 70

Common Questions

What's the minimum spend for private dining?

La Caja: $1,200 food & beverage minimum (lunch) / $1,800 (dinner). Main Room: $3,500 (lunch) / $5,000 (dinner). Buyouts vary by day and season. No room rental fee — only minimums.

Can we bring our own cake or decorations?

Yes! Outside cakes welcome (no cakeage fee). Simple decorations allowed — table runners, centerpieces, signage. No confetti, glitter, open flames, or wall attachments. Ask about our preferred florist partners.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Absolutely. Vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, nut-free — our kitchen handles these daily. We'll note restrictions on your final menu and our staff will guide guests. Advance notice (72 hrs) helps us prep properly.

How far in advance should I book?

Weekends book 4–8 weeks out. Weekdays often available with 2–3 weeks notice. Holiday season (Nov–Dec) books 3+ months ahead. Inquire early for best date selection.

What's the deposit / cancellation policy?

50% of estimated minimum due at signing. Cancellations: 30+ days = full refund; 14–29 days = 50% refund; <14 days = non-refundable. We'll work with you to reschedule if possible.

Can we do a tequila tasting or flight for our group?

Yes! Our certified agave specialists can curate a guided flight (3–5 pours) paired with your meal. $18–$35 per person depending on selections. Great for client entertainment or birthday celebrations.
