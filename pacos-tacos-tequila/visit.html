Reservations

Book Your Table

We accept reservations for parties of 2–12 via OpenTable. Larger groups should inquire about private dining.

  • Walk-ins welcome — bar and patio are first-come, first-served
  • Reservation window: 30 days in advance
  • Grace period: 15 minutes — please call if running late
  • Special requests: Booth, patio, high chair, accessibility — add notes when booking
  • Cancellations: Free up to 2 hours before; no-shows may affect future booking priority

Accessibility

Entrance & Navigation

  • Zero-step entry at both locations
  • Doorways 36"+ clear width
  • Accessible path to all dining areas, bar, patio, and restrooms
  • La Caja (SouthPark): accessible via main dining room

Restrooms

  • ADA-compliant stalls in all restrooms
  • Grab bars, proper sink height, touchless fixtures
  • Gender-neutral/family restroom available at SouthPark

Seating & Tables

  • Wheelchair-accessible tables throughout (27" knee clearance)
  • Booths with removable seats for flexible seating
  • Bar: accessible counter section with knee space
  • High chairs & boosters available

Sensory & Communication

  • Moderate noise level — peaks Fri/Sat 7–9 PM
  • Quietest: weekday lunch, Sunday dinner
  • Menus: large-print available on request
  • Staff trained to assist with menu reading
  • Service animals welcome per ADA

Parking & Arrival

  • SouthPark: 8 accessible spaces in shared lot (van-accessible)
  • Fort Mill: 6 accessible spaces in private lot (van-accessible)
  • Curbside drop-off at both entrances
  • Rideshare pickup/drop-off zones marked

Dietary & Allergies

  • Gluten-free corn tortillas (dedicated fryer for chips)
  • Vegetarian & vegan options marked on menu
  • Allergy protocols — inform server, manager will verify
  • Peanut-free kitchen (cross-contact possible)

Have a specific need not addressed here? Contact us — we're happy to discuss accommodations in advance.
