The Deliciousness Is in the Details

From the wood-fired grill to the top-shelf pour, every element at Paco's is intentional. Our menu showcases the freshest quality ingredients — each dish crafted under the expert guidance of James Beard award-winning Tex-Mex culinary authority and cookbook author Rob Walsh.

Central to the flavor-packed dining experience is our bar, featuring over 230 tequila varieties, several outstanding wines, and a curated selection of beers from Texas, Mexico, and the Carolinas.

Wood-Fired Grill

Fajitas grilled over real mesquite — the smoke penetrates every bite of marinated steak, chicken, and shrimp.

230+ Tequilas

Blanco, Reposado, Añejo, Extra Añejo, Cristalino — the deepest agave library in the Carolinas.

Private Dining

Two distinct spaces — La Caja for intimate gatherings, the Main Dining Room for celebrations up to 70.
