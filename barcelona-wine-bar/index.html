Spanish Tapas & Charcuterie

Welcome to Barcelona Wine Bar in South End Charlotte. We specialize in rustic small plates (tapas) inspired by the vibrant food culture of Spain and the Mediterranean. From wood-grilled octopus and crispy patatas bravas to hand-sliced jamón serrano and local artisan cheeses, our cellar kitchen prepares every plate to be shared alongside our extensive wine list.
