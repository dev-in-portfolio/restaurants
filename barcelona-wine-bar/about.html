Wood & Wine

Learn about our commitment to classic Spanish plancha techniques and our curated cellar program.

Cooking Over Planchas

At Barcelona Wine Bar, our tapas are driven by clean, intense heat. Our kitchen centers around the Spanish plancha—a flat metal griddle cooked over dry wood heat. Searing fresh seafood, ribeye steaks, and peppers at extremely high temperatures locks in clean flavors while producing a beautiful charred crust.

This simple, rustic cooking technique lets the natural quality of our local and imported ingredients speak for themselves.

America's Largest Spanish Wine List

Our cellar features nearly 400 unique selections, making it one of the largest Spanish wine programs in the United States. We focus heavily on regional Spanish varieties like dry Txakolina, crisp Albariño, earthy Mencía, and rich Rioja Tempranillos.

Whether you order a rare bottle or a flight of sherries, our staff is trained to help you discover the perfect glass to pair with your small plates.

Neighborhood Soul

Located in the heart of South End, our space blends industrial steel, rustic reclaimed oak wood, and cozy lighting to create the atmosphere of a bustling neighborhood bar in Barcelona. It is a place to gather, talk, pour wine, and pass small plates across the table.
