The Sanctuary

Our main dining room occupies the former church sanctuary, with soaring ceilings, original stained glass, and warm candlelit ambiance. The bar sits where the altar once stood — a nod to the building's reverence.

🍇

The Wine Cellar

Descend into the cellar for an intimate private dining experience. Stone walls, soft lighting, and a curated wine list make it the perfect setting for celebrations, business dinners, and special occasions.
