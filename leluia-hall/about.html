Our Story

From a congregation's first gathering in 1896 to a steak & surf dining room in 2026 — this building has always been about bringing people together.

We're located in a 1915 church building, on a plot of land where a congregation first began meeting in 1896, right on the edge of historic Dilworth and fast-growing Southend. A lot of groups — congregations and business owners alike — have called this place home over the years. We're delighted to be a part of its history, and now we all have the privilege to carry on the story.

1896

A congregation first gathers on this plot of land, planting the seeds of community at this corner of Dilworth.

1915

The church building is constructed — a craftsman-style structure with warm wood beams and a simple, reverent beauty that would endure for generations.

2024

Jeff & Jamie discover the vacant church. They envision a steak & surf concept that honors the building's history while bringing something entirely new to Charlotte.

2025

After a thoughtful restoration preserving original beams, stained glass, and the intimate sanctuary layout, Leluia Hall opens its doors.

Jeff & Jamie

Owners & Restaurateurs

Jeff and Jamie are a husband-and-wife team with deep roots in Charlotte's food scene — also behind Haberdish, Ever Andalo, and Supperland. Leluia Hall represents their most personal project yet, a tribute to sacred spaces and coastal flavors.

As a "steak & surf" concept, our menu at Leluia Hall is centered around prime cuts of steak, entrée seafoods, mini tacos with housemade tortillas, fresh takes on steakhouse sides, a full raw bar and thoughtful desserts. Flavors are fresh, bright and tropical with ingredients mostly inspired from the coastal Americas.

Whether you're here for a celebratory dinner, weekend brunch, or an intimate evening in our Wine Cellar, we hope you feel the warmth of a place that has welcomed the community for over a century.
