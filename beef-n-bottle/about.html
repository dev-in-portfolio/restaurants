Our Heritage

Understand our history, our iconic celebrity walls, and our dedication to preserving vintage steakhouse standards.

Established 1958

For over 65 years, Beef 'N Bottle has occupied its landmark spot on South Boulevard. Opened in 1958, our steakhouse has witnessed the evolution of Charlotte from a textile town to a banking center. Through it all, our dining room has remained largely untouched, preserving the intimate, cozy atmosphere that our guests expect.

The Celebrity Walls

Our wood-paneled walls tell a story of their own. Covered with hundreds of signed black-and-white headshots from actors, musicians, and athletes who have dined here over the decades, they offer a glimpse into the classic era of American dining. We maintain this collection as a tribute to our patrons.

Traditional Butchery

We maintain strict sourcing agreements to supply our kitchen. Our beef is premium aged choice and prime Angus, hand-cut on-site by our butchery team. We serve our steaks with classic steakhouse sides like red-skin garlic mashed potatoes and creamed spinach, preserving traditional culinary standards.
