A Charlotte Comfort Standard

Conveniently located on East Independence Boulevard in Charlotte, Queen's Soul Food is committed to serving hearty, authentic Southern dinner plates. Blending classic Georgia family seasonings with large comfort portions, our kitchen is renowned for fall-off-the-bone oxtails, smothered chops, and baked bakes.
