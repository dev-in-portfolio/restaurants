Our Southern Legacy

Serving Charlotte families authentic Southern soul dinners with a royal standard of excellence since 2012.

Independence Boulevard Landmark

Queen's Soul Food opened its doors in 2012 at 7308 E Independence Blvd, Charlotte. From day one, our mission was to provide East Charlotte with comforting, slow-cooked Southern cuisine that makes everyone feel at home.

Our dining rooms have welcomed generations of local families for Sunday bakes, corporate lunches, and takeout meals, serving as a neighborhood gathering point.

The Royal Standard of Seasoning

We call it the "Royal Standard" because we believe soul food should never be rushed or half-cooked. We hand-select our beef oxtails, double-dredge our chicken wings in seasoned flour, and slow-cook our collard greens with smoked turkey wing broth for rich savory depth.

All our sides, including our four-cheese baked macaroni and sweetened candied yams, are baked fresh every single morning.

Double-Layer Baking Traditions

No dinner plate is complete without a slice of homemade cake. Our bakers keep Southern dessert traditions alive, preparing double-layered strawberry, coconut, and fudge chocolate cakes from scratch. One slice is massive, moist, and cooked with love.
