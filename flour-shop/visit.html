Creekside Patio

Our creekside patio is one of Charlotte's hidden gems. Dine under the trees in the backlot of Park Road Shopping Center — a peaceful, shaded setting perfect for warm evenings. Patio seating is available on a first-come, first-served basis, and we recommend requesting it when you reserve.

Reservations

We take reservations through OpenTable. Walk-ins are welcome when available, but we recommend booking ahead — especially for the pasta bar seats and weekend evenings.

Book on OpenTable Call (980) 299-3754

Parking

Free parking is available in the public lot at Park Road Shopping Center. Enter from Brandywine Road and follow signs to the backlot. Additional street parking is available along Brandywine Road.
