A Pasta Bar with a View of the Kitchen

At Flour Shop, Chef Trey Wilson brings the garden and the kitchen together in Charlotte's Park Road Shopping Center. Our 24-seat pasta bar puts you front and center — watch handmade pasta come to life at the open kitchen, then enjoy it by the creek on our patio or in our warm, intimate dining room. Every dish starts with ingredients sourced from local farms and fishermen, and plenty from Chef Trey's own backyard garden.
