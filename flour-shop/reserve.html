Book on OpenTable

We partner with OpenTable for all reservations. Select your party size, date, and time — you'll be seated in our dining room, at the pasta bar, or on the creekside patio (weather permitting).

Walk-Ins Welcome

We hold a portion of our seats for walk-in guests. If you don't have a reservation, come by and we'll do our best to seat you. The pasta bar is often the best spot for walk-ins — grab a seat and watch the kitchen work.

Creekside Patio

Our patio seating is available on a first-come, first-served basis. Request patio seating when you book, or ask your server when you arrive. The patio is beautiful on warm evenings — shaded, quiet, and right along the creek.

What to Expect

  • Open Kitchen — Watch your meal come together at the pasta bar
  • 24 Pasta Bar Seats — The best seats in the house, up close to the action
  • Creekside Patio — Shaded outdoor dining in the backlot
  • Seasonal Menu — Every visit is a little different

Date Night

Flour Shop is one of Charlotte's favorite date night spots. The intimate dining room, open kitchen, and creekside patio set the mood. Start with Burrata Di Bufala, share the Squid Ink Linguine, and linger over dessert.
