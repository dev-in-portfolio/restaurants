Our Story

Locally owned. Locally operated. Crafted with pride.

How Duckworth's Began

Duckworth's Grill & Taphouse was built on a simple philosophy — to create non-pretentious menu items and offer superb beer selections that can't be found just anywhere. What started as a single location in Charlotte has grown into a collection of neighborhood gathering spots across the Carolinas, each one owned and operated by local families.

The name Duckworth's reflects a commitment to quality without the attitude. Every dish is made from scratch using fresh ingredients, and every tap line is curated to bring guests beers they won't find at just any sports bar. From the Uptown flagship at 330 Tryon to locations in SouthPark, Mooresville, Huntersville, and Rea Farms, each Duckworth's is its own community hub.
