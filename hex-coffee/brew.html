Brew Guides

Simple, repeatable recipes for light-roast coffee.

☕ V60 Pour-Over

18g coffee · 300g water · 2:30 total
  1. Rinse filter, add 18g ground coffee (medium-fine).
  2. Bloom with 50g water, wait 35s.
  3. Pour to 180g in slow spirals.
  4. Final pour to 300g. Drawdown by 2:30.

🧊 Cold Brew

100g coffee · 900g water · 16h steep
  1. Coarse grind 100g coffee.
  2. Combine with filtered water in a jar.
  3. Steep 16 hours at room temp.
  4. Filter through paper. Serve over ice.

🫖 AeroPress

15g coffee · 250g water · 1:30
  1. Fine grind. Add 15g coffee + 250g water at 205°F.
  2. Stir gently for 10s.
  3. Press slowly — 30s total.
