House-Made Milk Bread

The bread that built a reputation

Made using the tangzhong method, our milk bread is a labor of love — pillowy, slightly sweet, and impossibly soft. Served warm with house-cultured butter and sea salt. It's the first thing guests taste and the dish they remember most.

An Unforgettable Setting

Housed in a former pharmacy on Main Street, Kindred blends crisp white tiles, exposed brick, and hardwood floors with mismatched plates and flatware that make every table feel like home.

The Space
