Parking in Davidson

  • Free street parking on Main Street and surrounding blocks
  • Public lot behind the restaurant (enter from Depot Street)
  • Additional parking at Davidson Town Hall lot (216 S Main St)
  • Handicap accessible entrance available
  • Rideshare drop-off at front door

From Charlotte

Take I-77 N to Exit 30 (Davidson). Follow signs to downtown — Main Street runs along the college. Kindred is at 131 N Main St, on the right, identifiable by its crisp white tile and warm wood facade.
