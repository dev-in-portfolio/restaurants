Mortal Philosophy

Understand the origins of our Asheville brewing collective, our Plaza Midwood sanctuary, and the cycle of decay.

Roots in the Blue Ridge

Burial Beer Co. was founded in Asheville, NC in 2013, seeking to celebrate the cycle of life, death, and decay through the art of fermentation. We believe that brewing is an expression of mortal struggle—taking ingredients from the earth, aging them in oak staves, and letting yeast perform its transformative decay.

Charlotte House of Relics

Opened as our Charlotte outpost in Plaza Midwood, the "House of Relics" is an immersive sanctuary. We preserved the bones of a historic Thomas Avenue house, lining the walls with aged oak staves, gothic paintings, and custom gold-cast taps.

The Art of the Label

Our labels are notorious for their detailed, dark oil paintings and prose. We treat every can as a canvas to share reflections on human mortality, history, and gratitude, creating a dense sensory experience that goes beyond the pint.
