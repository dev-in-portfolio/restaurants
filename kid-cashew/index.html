The Freshest Kitchen in Charlotte

We don't own a freezer. We don't own a fryer. We don't own a heat lamp. Every plate that leaves our pass was cooked to order over an open wood fire or prepped fresh that morning. It's not a gimmick — it's the only way we know how to cook.

Located at 1608 East Blvd in the heart of Dilworth and South End, Kid Cashew is a gathering place for neighbors, families, and anyone who believes a great meal starts with great ingredients.
