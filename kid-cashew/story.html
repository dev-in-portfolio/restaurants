Our Story

How a little cashew became a neighborhood staple.

Born in Dilworth

In the heart of Charlotte's Dilworth and South End corridor, 1608 East Blvd became home to something different. Kid Cashew opened with a simple idea: serve honest, wood-fired food that brings people together. No concepts. No gimmicks. Just great ingredients cooked over live fire.

The name comes from the cashew fruit — a small, humble thing that holds tremendous flavor. It's a reminder that the best things often come in unassuming packages.

"Sharing Flavors & Laughter"

— the Kid Cashew way

The Philosophy

❄️

No Freezer

We source fresh daily. No frozen inventory means every ingredient has to earn its place on our menu.

🍟

No Fryer

Everything comes from our wood fire grill. No fryer baskets, no reheated oil — just live fire cooking.

💡

No Heat Lamps

Every plate is cooked to order. It leaves our pass the moment it's ready, straight to your table.
