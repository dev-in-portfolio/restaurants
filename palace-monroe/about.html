The Palace Heritage

Serving Monroe families hearty home-style diner breakfasts and dinners on Lancaster Avenue.

Monroe Roadside Diner Standard

The Palace Restaurant opened its doors on Lancaster Avenue to serve Monroe with classic, comforting diner food. From day one, we set out to make a diner where friends, families, and neighbors could gather for a hot cup of coffee, buttered toast, and sizzling bacon.

Our dining rooms features vintage cozy booths and a classic register counter, capturing the timeless spirit of American roadside diners.

Fresh Eggs & Homemade Gravies

Every morning, our grill cooks crack fresh farm eggs to order, flip fluffy buttermilk pancake batter, and brown country sausages. For dinner, we seared fresh hamburger steak patties and simmer gravies from scratch using rich pan-drippings.

Everything we serve is prepared with a standard of home-cooked simplicity and generous comfort portions.

Three Generations of Comfort

For decades, our Lancaster Avenue dining room has welcomed generations of local families. Grandparents who dined with us in the late 90s now bring their grandkids to enjoy warm blueberry pancakes, country biscuits, and ribeye dinners. We thank you for letting us feed your family.
