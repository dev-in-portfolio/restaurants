Home Cooking on Lancaster Ave

Welcome to The Palace Restaurant in Monroe, NC. For decades, our kitchen on Lancaster Avenue has served generous portions of classic diner breakfasts, lunch sandwiches, and hearty dinner bakes. Every egg is cracked fresh, our gravies are made from pan-drippings, and our pancakes are whipped from butter batter.
