Wood-Fire & Scratch Prep

Understand our open-kitchen concepts, wood-grilling heritage, and scratch-made culinary standards.

Open Kitchen & Hickory Grill

Our dining rooms are designed around an open-concept kitchen. Guests can see the flames of our hickory wood-fire grill, watch chefs pull sizzling skillets of cornbread from the deck ovens, and see fresh Atlantic salmon arranged on cedar planks. We believe transparency is key to an honest culinary experience.

Scratch-Made Daily

Every dressing, marinade, sauce, and dessert is made completely from scratch in our kitchens every single morning. From our signature honey vinaigrettes to our key lime pie fillings and hand-rolled crab cakes, we use fresh, premium ingredients to ensure deep flavor profiles.

Heritage Farming

We maintain strict sourcing agreements to supply our locations. Our beef is USDA choice or prime Angus, dry-aged and hand-cut. Our seafood is caught under sustainable standards, delivered directly to our SouthPark and Blakeney dock doors, and portioned fresh by our butchery team.
