Welcome to Haberdish

A mill town southern kitchen and craft cocktail bar in NoDa

Join us for Carolina-inspired cuisine with crispy fried chicken, southern vegetables and locally inspired side plates. Our bartenders serve seasonally crafted apothecary cocktails from a 1950s soda fountain — perfectly paired with our food and southern climate. Michelin Guide 2025 Selected.

🍗

Best Fried Chicken

Second only to your mom or grandmother's — crispy, seasoned, and served with southern love.

🍸

Apothecary Cocktails

Seasonal craft cocktails shaken, stirred, or poured from our 1950s soda fountain bar.

Jumble of Plates

Family-style dining — meats, vegetables, and sides served together like a potluck with only your favorites.

Mill Town Heritage

In a historic NoDa mill building with salvaged denim, repurposed mill spindles, and local artisan touches.

Michelin Guide 2025

Haberdish has been recognized by the Michelin Guide as a Selected restaurant — a testament to the team's dedication to Carolina-inspired cuisine, craft cocktails, and genuine hospitality in Charlotte's historic mill district.

Created by Jeff Tonidandel and Jamie Brown — the team behind Ever Andalo, Growlers Pourhouse, Reigning Doughnuts, and Supperland.
