Our Story

Where it all started for Rare Roots Hospitality

From Dream to Destination

Dressler's Restaurant is where it all started for Rare Roots Hospitality Group. Chasing a lifelong dream, Kim and Jon Dressler opened the first Dressler's in Huntersville in 2003, and expanded to add the location at The Metropolitan in 2010. For over two decades, Dressler's has existed to provide memorable evenings and exceptional service.

Named one of Charlotte's best steakhouses and seafood destinations, Dressler's combines prime cuts of beef with imaginative seafood preparations, an extensive wine list, and a warm, sophisticated atmosphere. The Metropolitan location features an expansive patio with one of the best views of the Uptown Charlotte skyline, nestled along the Little Sugar Creek Greenway.

2003

Kim and Jon Dressler open the first Dressler's in Huntersville's Birkdale Village

2010

Dressler's Metropolitan opens at The Metropolitan in Midtown Charlotte

2015+

Rare Roots Hospitality Group expands with new concepts while Dressler's remains the flagship

Today

Dressler's continues to serve Charlotte with prime steaks, fresh seafood, and skyline patio dining

Part of the Rare Roots Hospitality family — which includes Fin & Fino, Chapter 6, The Porter's House, and more — Dressler's remains the cornerstone of the group's commitment to exceptional dining experiences.
