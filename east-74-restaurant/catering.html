Catering & Large Orders

East 74 for your next gathering

Feed a Crowd the East 74 Way

Planning a family reunion, office lunch, church event, or birthday party? Let East 74 handle the food. We offer party platters, breakfast packages, and large takeout orders made fresh with the same homestyle quality our regulars love.

All catering and large orders are prepared fresh. Give us a heads up so we can have everything ready when you need it.

What We Offer

Breakfast Catering

Feed your morning crowd with omelet platters, country breakfasts, biscuits and gravy, and coffee service. Perfect for meetings and church groups.

  • Omelet platters (assorted varieties)
  • Country breakfast trays
  • Biscuits and sausage gravy
  • Coffee and juice service

Party Platters

Bring our favorites to your event. Gyros, fried favorites, sandwiches, and sides — enough to keep everyone happy.

  • Gyro meat platter with pita and toppings
  • Fried chicken tenders
  • Fried squash and okra trays
  • Party-sized salads

Takeout Orders

Need 5 or 50 meals? Call ahead and we'll have your order boxed and ready for pickup. Great for offices, families, and events.

  • Individual and group takeout
  • Family meal deals
  • Custom orders welcome
  • Advance notice appreciated
