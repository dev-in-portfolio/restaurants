Our Story

A Matthews neighborhood diner since 2013

Where Monroe Road Meets Home

East 74 Restaurant is a family-owned diner sitting right on Monroe Road — Route 74 — in the heart of Matthews, North Carolina. The name says it all: we're right here on the road, easy to find, and even easier to love.

Since opening our doors in 2013, we've been serving up honest, homestyle American cooking with a Greek twist. Our menu reflects the traditions that make a diner feel like home — fluffy omelets, crispy fried favorites, and hearty platters — alongside Greek staples like gyros, Greek salads, and homemade baklava.

With about a dozen employees who know most customers by name, East 74 isn't a big operation. It's the kind of place where the server remembers how you like your eggs, where families stop in after church on Sunday, and where you can get a full meal at a price that doesn't sting.

We're proud to be a gathering place for the Matthews community — a spot where the coffee's always fresh, the portions are generous, and everybody's welcome. That's what a neighborhood diner should be.
