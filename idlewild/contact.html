Frequently Asked Questions

Do you really not have a menu? +

That's right. Our bartenders will ask about your taste preferences — sweet, herbal, smoky, spirit-forward — and build a custom cocktail on the spot. No menu, no decisions, just discovery.

Do you serve food? +

We offer a small bites menu designed to complement our cocktails — think marinated olives, charcuterie, warm snacks, and seasonal plates. Perfect for sharing.

Is there a dress code? +

We encourage a date-night / night-out vibe but there's no strict dress code. Come as you are — just bring good energy.

Can I host a private event? +

We can accommodate buyouts and private events for groups. Reach out through the contact form or call us to discuss availability.

Do you have non-alcoholic options? +

Absolutely. Our bartenders create zero-proof cocktails with the same care and creativity as our spirit-based drinks. Just let them know your preference.
