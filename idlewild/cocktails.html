Spirit Guide

A quick reference to the spirits our bartenders love to work with. Not sure where to start? Just say the word.

  • Vodka

    Clean and versatile. The perfect canvas for bright citrus, herbs, or a classic martini experience.

    Clean Versatile

  • Gin

    Botanical and aromatic. Our bar carries both London dry and contemporary craft gins for every palate.

    Botanical Herbal

  • Whiskey

    From smooth bourbons to smoky Scotches and experimental American single malts. Depth in every pour.

    Bold Warm

  • Rum

    Not just tropical. Aged rums bring caramel, oak, and spice that rival any whiskey in complexity.

    Rich Complex

  • Tequila & Mezcal

    Agave spirits range from bright and crisp to deeply smoky. A favorite for creative cocktails.

    Earthy Smoky
