The Art of the Crêpe

European-style craftsmanship meets the warm neighborhood charm of Downtown Matthews.

European Inspiration

Founded in 2018 in historic Downtown Matthews, Royal Cafe & Creperie was born out of a desire to bring the authentic streetside crêperies of Paris and the artisanal gelato cafes of Rome to the local Charlotte community. We wanted to create a cozy, elegant space where guests could watch their crêpes spun fresh on iron griddles.

Our open-kitchen concept allows the sweet aroma of butter and sugar to fill the air, welcoming customers to slow down and savor their morning.

Scratch Batter & Fresh Fruits

We believe simplicity requires the finest ingredients. Our signature crêpe batters (including our organic gluten-free and vegan alternatives) are prepared from scratch every morning. We source organic strawberries, bananas, fresh baby spinach, local goat cheese, and premium cured meats daily.

Our espresso bar features custom-roasted Arabica beans pulled through antique copper machines, ensuring every cup of latte or macchiato is perfect.

Matthews Sweet Spot

More than just a café, Royal Cafe is a place of community. From weekend brunch seekers and families sharing a gelato bowl to neighborhood morning clubs, we pride ourselves on being a sweet anchor in Matthews. We welcome you to find a cozy booth, sip a latte, and enjoy the delicate art of the crêpe.
