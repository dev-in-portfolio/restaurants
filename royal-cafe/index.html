Cozy Cafe Comforts

Welcome to Royal Cafe & Creperie. Located in historic Downtown Matthews, we specialize in authentic French-style sweet and savory crêpes spun fresh to order. Pair your crêpe with our custom-roasted espresso beans, premium loose-leaf herbal teas, or a refreshing scoop of artisanal Italian gelato. Vegan and gluten-free batter selections are proudly provided.
