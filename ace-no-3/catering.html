Catering FAQs

Is there an order minimum?
Nope! Order what you are craving and we will make it happen.

Yes, each store offers delivery within a 5-mile radius for $25, and 5–8 miles for $35.
Can you accommodate dietary needs?
Yes, we have several gluten-free and veggie options available.
How far in advance should I order?
We recommend at least 24 hours’ notice for catering orders, but we’ll do our best to accommodate shorter notice.
