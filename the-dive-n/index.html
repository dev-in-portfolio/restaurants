The Heart of Pineville

Located on North Polk Street in historic downtown Pineville, The Dive N is the neighborhood's favorite old-school diner. We serve up massive breakfast plates, classic diner melts, and Southern burgers from our hot griddle. Known for quick service, friendly chatter, and simple diner comfort.
