21+ Upscale Sports Lounge

Blinders Sports Lounge was founded to fill a gap in Charlotte's dining scene: an upscale watch bar that takes food and drinks as seriously as it takes the game. Operating strictly as a 21+ venue, we offer sports fans a refined environment to cheer, socialize, and dine.

No sticky floors or generic pitchers—just comfortable leather seating, custom craft cocktails, and high-end company.

Calibrated HD Screen & Audio Arrays

Every screen at Blinders is calibrated for color accuracy and high contrast, ensuring the ball is visible from any corner of the room. We deploy professional directional audio arrays at select tables, allowing groups to listen to their preferred broadcast without background dining noise.

A Gourmet Take on Stadium Classics

Our kitchen rejects standard frozen sports pub menus. From our signature wings fried in 100% duck fat to double-smashed angus beef slider patties and hand-rolled flatbreads, our culinary team uses fresh, local Carolina ingredients to elevate classic bar fare into premium dinners.
