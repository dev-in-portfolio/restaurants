The NoDa Landmark

For over fifty years, Brooks' Sandwich House has stood at the corner of Brevard and 30th Streets. Long before NoDa became Charlotte's premier arts district, we were serving truck drivers, railway workers, and neighbors. Our recipe is simple: premium meats, soft buns, and our famous signature homemade beef chili.
