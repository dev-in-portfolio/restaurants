Retro Soul & Hops

Learn about our quirky mascot, neighborhood roots, and our collection of vintage arcade cabinets.

Who is Bart?

Unlike typical stuffy craft shops, Bart’s Mart doesn’t take itself too seriously. Named in honor of a quirky, cartoonish neighborhood spirit, our mascot "Bart" represents the skater-chic, retro-loving soul of the Eastway and Plaza-Midwood communities.

Our shelves are stocked with rare local releases, but our lounge is packed with laughter, free board games, and cheap snacks.

Plaza-Midwood & Eastway Crossings

Founded as a social bridge in the Eastway Crossing shopping center, Bart’s Mart was created to be a dedicated hub where neighbors could chat, share a bottle, or order takeout to eat on our deck. We support local North Carolina breweries and small-batch wineries.

The Retro Arcade Corner

A primary highlight of our taproom is the retro gaming corner. From vintage pinball machines and cabinet classics like Street Fighter and Pac-Man to modern board game stacks, we encourage guests to grab a glass, grab a joystick, and settle old neighborhood rivalries the old-fashioned way.
