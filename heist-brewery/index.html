Born in Charlotte — The Original Hazy IPA

In 2016, Heist Brewery created CitraQuench'l — the first hazy IPA in North Carolina. Our portfolio has since grown to include pilsners, fruited sours, adjunct stouts, and barrel-aged specialties.
