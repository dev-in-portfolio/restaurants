Our Story

The story of Charlotte's original craft brewpub

From a 10HL German Brewhouse

Heist Brewery opened its doors in 2012 as Charlotte's only craft brewpub. What started on a small 10HL German brewhouse has grown into a 30-barrel production facility, but our commitment to small-batch quality has never changed.

Our original focus was on traditional Belgian and German-style beers — crisp pilsners, rich dubbels, and complex tripels. Then in 2016, everything changed. We created CitraQuench'l, the original hazy IPA in North Carolina, and found our stride.

"In 2016, we created the original hazy IPA in North Carolina — CitraQuench'l."

Today, our portfolio spans hazy IPAs, pilsners, fruited sours, adjunct stouts, and barrel-aged specialties. We still brew small batches that are kept in-house, giving our taproom regulars something unique with every visit.

But Heist is more than beer. Our food program has evolved through many iterations, always centered around wood-fired specialties. Our flatbreads and dips are our pride and joy. Our burgers use a proprietary hand-selected blend of meats you won't find anywhere else. Our chefs source produce and meats from local farms and incorporate ingredients from the brewing process itself.

And our cocktail program is forever evolving — from simple and refreshing to complex and thought-provoking. No matter the occasion, you'll find something to enjoy.

The Timeline

2012

Heist Brewery opens in NoDa as Charlotte's only craft brewpub, brewing on a 10HL German brewhouse with a focus on Belgian and German styles.

2016

CitraQuench'l launches — the first hazy IPA in North Carolina. Heist finds its signature style and begins expanding its portfolio.

2017

Production expands to a 30-barrel facility, allowing Heist to distribute across the region while maintaining small-batch quality.

2020

Barrel Arts at North End opens, featuring Livy's Neapolitan Pizzeria, craft cocktails, and a spacious outdoor patio.

Today

Two Charlotte locations, a full barrel-aging program, distribution partnerships, and a dedicated following. Still independent, still brewing fearlessly.
