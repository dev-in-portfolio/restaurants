📚 Our Story

About Crispy Banh Mi

Spreading Vietnamese culture through culinary, one sandwich at a time.

Crispy Banh Mi debuted in Charlotte in 2016 with a simple mission: bring authentic Vietnamese bánh mì to the Queen City. What started as a single location on South Blvd has grown to three restaurants across Charlotte — each one serving the same golden, crispy baguettes filled with fresh ingredients.

The Food

Our restaurant specializes in Vietnamese sandwiches (Bánh Mì), desserts (Che) and smoothies. We cook our food and cut our vegetables fresh every day to bring the freshest food. Our smoothies and teas are also blended and brewed fresh daily.

The Bread

Every bánh mì starts with the bread. Our golden, crispy French baguettes have the perfect crunch on the outside and soft, airy interior. It's the foundation of everything we do — and the reason customers keep coming back.

The Culture

We love to spread our Vietnamese culture through culinary. From the classic Grilled Pork bánh mì to traditional Che desserts and Vietnamese coffee, every item on our menu tells a story of Vietnamese flavors and traditions.
