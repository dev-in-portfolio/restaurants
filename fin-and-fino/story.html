Our Story

Explorer's mindset. Sailor's heart. A social seafood house for Charlotte.

Fin & Fino opened its doors in Uptown Charlotte in 2018 with a simple belief: that the best meals are shared, discovered and celebrated together. The name itself — "Fin" for the seafaring explorer and "Fino" for the refined palate — reflects a dual identity: adventurous yet approachable, thoughtful yet playful.

Founded by Rare Roots Hospitality, the same family behind Dressler's, Chapter Six and other beloved Charlotte concepts, Fin & Fino was designed as a social seafood house — a place where guests can shake off a busy day or shake up a slow one. The menu draws inspiration from seafaring cultures on both sides of the Atlantic, celebrating fresh seafood, high-quality meats, house-made pastas and locally grown produce.

Every visit offers something new to be explored and enjoyed. From the rotating cocktail program (now on its 12th menu, "PLOT TWIST") to the daily oyster selection and the ever-changing "Call of the Clam" custom cocktail creations, Fin & Fino rewards curiosity and repeat visits.

In 2024, Fin & Fino expanded to a second location at Birkdale Village in Huntersville, bringing the same social seafood energy to the Lake Norman area. Today, the brand continues to evolve — new menus, new cocktails, new adventures — while staying rooted in the warmth, quality and discovery that made it a Charlotte favorite.
