About Curry Gate

Bringing authentic Indian and Nepali flavors to the Charlotte community.

A Family Tradition

Curry Gate was born from a simple vision: to share the rich, diverse flavors of Indian and Nepali cuisine with the Charlotte community. What began as a single restaurant serving family recipes has grown into five locations across the metro area, each rooted in the same commitment to authentic spices and fresh ingredients.

Our menu draws from both Indian and Nepali culinary traditions. From the creamy Butter Chicken to the delicate momos filled with spiced vegetables, every dish is prepared with care and served with warmth.
