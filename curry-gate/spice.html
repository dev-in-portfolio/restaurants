Spice Level Details

Level 1 - 2 Mild

Perfect for those who prefer aroma over heat. Rich coconut cream, cashew paste, and light cardamom flavors dominate the palate.

Level 3 - 4 Medium

A pleasant, warm kick of red chili powder and fresh ginger. Ideal for first-time guests looking to explore authentic Indian flavors.

Level 5 - 6 Indian Hot

The standard spice level preferred by our kitchen. A heavy dose of fresh green chilies and house garam masala.

Level 10+ Volcanic

Not for the faint of heart. Spiced with Ghost Pepper and Carolina Reaper chili flakes. Searing heat, proceed with caution.
