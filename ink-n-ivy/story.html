Our Story

Written in Ink. Still Growing like Ivy.

The Bottlecap Group

Ink N Ivy is proud to be part of the Bottlecap Group, a Charlotte hospitality collective behind some of the city’s most beloved spots — including All American Pub, Whiskey Warehouse, and others. We believe in creating spaces where people come together, share a meal, and make memories.

Every concept in the group has its own personality, but they all share one thing: a commitment to great food, thoughtful drinks, and genuine hospitality.

“Eat, Drink n Be Social”

The Space

Design & Details

Button-tucked booths, handcrafted wood slab tables, and chandeliers hanging from soaring ceilings. The space blends industrial edge with Southern warmth.

The Patio

Our patio overlooks Romare Bearden Park with an unobstructed view of the Charlotte skyline. The perfect spot for sunset drinks, brunch, or post-game celebrations.

Art & Atmosphere

Funky local artwork and curated vintage touches are woven throughout the space. Every corner has something to discover — just like Uptown itself.

Uptown Charlotte

We’re at 222 S Church St, across from Romare Bearden Park and just steps from Bank of America Stadium and Truist Field. Whether you’re heading to a Hornets game, Panthers tailgate, or just exploring Uptown, we’re your home base.

The neighborhood is alive with energy — and so are we. From the pre-game rush to late-night laughs, Ink N Ivy is where Uptown comes to eat, drink, and connect.
