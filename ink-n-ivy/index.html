Uptown Energy. Timeless Vibe.

Ink N Ivy sits at the corner of 222 S Church Street, steps from Romare Bearden Park with a patio that puts the Charlotte skyline front and center. Button-tucked booths, wood slab tables, and high-ceiling chandeliers set the stage for a night out.

Big screens, local artwork, and a lively atmosphere make this the game day destination in Uptown — whether you’re here for Hornets, Panthers, or football.
