Welcome to Harper's Cafe

Fast-casual breakfast & lunch in Pineville

Harper's Cafe is a breakfast and lunch fast-casual restaurant located in the Terrace at Park Place in Pineville. We bring back the classics you've missed — Chicken Supremes, Original Oriental salad, pesto pasta salad, and Black Bean Soup — alongside new breakfast items and rotating weekly signatures.

🍗

Chicken Supremes

The legend returns — crispy chicken tenderloins served with your choice of sauce and sides.

🥗

Original Oriental Salad

A Harper's signature for over three decades — fresh greens, crispy chicken, and that unforgettable dressing.

🍳

Breakfast All Day

Omelettes, breakfast bowls, burritos, and sandwiches — served until 3 PM daily.

🍝

Pesto Pasta Salad

House-made pesto, fresh pasta, and the perfect blend of flavors — a Charlotte original.

Now Open for Dinner

Harper's Cafe is now open for early dinner on Friday and Saturday evenings, plus Brunch on Sundays. Come enjoy your favorites in a relaxed fast-casual setting with the same hospitality Charlotte has counted on for 35 years.
