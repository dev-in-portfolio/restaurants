Our Story

How It All Began

From Woodlawn Road to Pineville — a Charlotte tradition

Harper's opened its doors in Charlotte in December of 1987 on Woodlawn Road. For decades, it was a beloved gathering place for Charlotteans seeking classic American fare, warm hospitality, and a menu full of signature dishes that became local legends. Harper's SouthPark opened in September of 1992, further cementing the family of restaurants as a Charlotte staple.

1987

1992

2000s–2020s
Decades of serving Chicken Supremes, Oriental salad, Black Bean Soup — dishes that defined Charlotte dining
May 2025
Harper's Cafe opens quietly at the Terrace at Park Place in Pineville — bringing back old favorites and introducing new breakfast items
Today
Now open for breakfast, lunch, early dinner, and Sunday brunch — still all about hospitality and quality
