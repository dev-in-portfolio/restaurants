The Dinner Exhibition

A seasonal, locally-sourced menu designed by our culinary team to complement the boutique wines and craft spirits in our cellar.

Curated Plates

Truffle Frites $14

Crisp thin-cut fries tossed with Pecorino Romano cheese, fresh rosemary sprigs, and served with a rich side of roasted garlic aioli.

Char-Grilled Shrimp & Grits $24

Fresh jumbo shrimp char-grilled, layered over creamy local stone-ground yellow grits, topped with a smoky tasso ham aioli.

PG Signature Burger $20

10oz custom prime beef blend, sweet caramelized onion jam, aged white cheddar, crispy applewood bacon, fresh baby arugula, on toasted brioche.

Maple Brussels Sprouts $12

Pan-seared Brussels sprout halves tossed with applewood bacon lardons and drizzled with sweet maple-balsamic reduction.

Artisanal Flatbread $16

Char-grilled flatbread crust, fig jam spread, gorgonzola cheese crumbles, sliced prosciutto di Parma, topped with dressed baby arugula.

Liquid Masterpieces

The Mona Lisa $16

Botanical-infused gin, fresh elderflower liqueur, rosemary simple syrup, freshly squeezed lemon juice, floated with crisp Prosecco.

The Charles Street $18

Small-batch rye whiskey, sweet vermouth, smoked orange bitters, brandied cherry, stirred and poured over a clear slow-melting ice sphere.

The Hidden Gem $15

Premium spiced rum, fresh muddled local blackberries, squeezed lime juice, bubbly ginger beer, finished with a fresh mint bouquet.

Victorian Garden (Zero Proof) $10

Seedlip herbal non-alcoholic spirit, muddled fresh cucumber, basil syrup, fresh lime juice, club soda splash.
