Eggs, Legs, and Kegs

Located on East Franklin Street in historic downtown Monroe, E.L.K. is a modern tavern serving made-from-scratch pub classics. Our name stands for **Eggs** (our signature deviled eggs), **Legs** (crispy jumbo chicken wings), and **Kegs** (our rotating NC craft drafts and specialty cocktails).
