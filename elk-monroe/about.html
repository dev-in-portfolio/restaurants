The E.L.K. Chronicles

Monroe's modern tavern landmark, serving scratch-made Southern favorites in historic downtown.

Revitalizing Downtown Monroe

E.L.K. Bar & Restaurant opened in 2022 inside a beautifully restored brick building on the corner of 301 E. Franklin Street in historic downtown Monroe, NC. Combining rustic tavern brick walls with modern steel finishes, our space is designed for relaxing pub dining.

Whether you sit at our custom timber bar or tuck into a cozy dining room booth, E.L.K. welcomes you to eat, drink, and socialize.

What is E.L.K.?

While our name inspires our mascot, E.L.K. stands for **Eggs, Legs, and Kegs**, representing our primary menu anchors. It also stands for our ultimate hospitality goal: **Eat Like a King**.

From creamy whipped deviled egg appetizers (Eggs) and golden-fried crispy chicken wings (Legs) to local North Carolina craft draft beer (Kegs), our tavern board represents modern comfort.

The Scratch Kitchen Standard

Every item that leaves our kitchen is made from scratch. We grind our own custom Angus beef burger patties, wrap our Scotch eggs in custom seasoned sausage meat, and whisk our honey-paprika yolk cream daily. We partner with Monroe and Charlotte micro-breweries to guarantee fresh, rotating keg selections.
