The Heart of Lang Van

A family staple in East Charlotte, serving comfort food and legendary hospitality for over two decades.

A Charlotte Institution Since 1999

Lang Van opened its doors in 1999 on Shamrock Drive in East Charlotte. At the time, authentic Vietnamese cuisine was hard to find in the city. Our founders set out to build a simple, unpretentious dining room focused purely on clean, healthy, and highly traditional recipes.

Over the years, as Charlotte rapidly grew, Lang Van remained a comforting, unchanging anchor. We still serve our original recipes, prepared with fresh herbs and spices daily.

The Soul of the Parlor: Dan Nguyen

Ask any regular at Lang Van why they return week after week, and they will mention owner Dan Nguyen. Dan has run the dining floor for decades and is famous throughout Charlotte for her incredible memory and warm, familial affection.

She memorizes the names, families, and favorite orders of hundreds of regular customers. If you are undecided, Dan will look at you, ask a few questions about your taste preferences, and curate a customized order that is guaranteed to hit the spot. At Lang Van, you are not just a customer—you are an expected guest in Dan's dining room.

Michelin Bib Gourmand Recognition

In recognition of our long-standing devotion to quality, Lang Van was recently honored by the Michelin Guide with a **Bib Gourmand** designation. This honor recognizes establishments that serve exceptional food at moderate prices. While we are proud of this recognition, we remain dedicated to our roots—serving simple, authentic bowls of pho and fresh spring rolls with the same humble, community-first values we started with in 1999.
