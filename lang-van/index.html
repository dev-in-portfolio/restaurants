Welcome to the Lang Van Family

"For over two decades, we have served fresh, authentic, and comforting Vietnamese cooking to our Charlotte community, welcoming everyone who walks through our doors like family."

Fresh Herbs & Rich Traditions

Lang Van is a beloved culinary institution in East Charlotte. Famous for our extensive menu of over 100 authentic Vietnamese recipes, we emphasize fresh herbs, light vermicelli bowls, comforting clay pots, and deeply aromatic beef broth slow-simmered for hours. Guided by owner Dan Nguyen, we dedicate ourselves to serving healthy, flavorful food with genuine heart.

House Favorites

Appetizer
Gỏi Cuốn (Summer Rolls) $8.50

Fresh rice paper rolls wrapped around lean shrimp, sliced pork, vermicelli rice noodles, lettuce, mint, and chives. Served cold with our signature house-made peanut dipping sauce.

Signature
Phở Đặc Biệt (Beef Combo) $16.50

Our legendary aromatic beef noodle soup. A rich broth filled with thin slices of ribeye, flank steak, brisket, and beef meatballs. Served with a side plate of fresh bean sprouts, basil, lime, and jalapeños.
