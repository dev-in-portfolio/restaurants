Fiesta Catering & To-Go

Feed your corporate meeting, airport shift crew, or weekend tailgate party with Miguel's massive portion trays.

Fajita Party Trays

Bring our sizzling skillet flavor to your group. Massive party pans of grilled steak, chicken, and shrimp cooked with bell peppers and sweet onions. Complete with side pans of beans, rice, and tortillas. Serves 15 to 20.

Corporate Lunch Boxes

Perfect for office luncheons. We package individual burritos, chimichangas, or cheeseburger baskets wrapped hot in foil and boxed with chips and salsa. Call ahead for group orders over 10.

Taco Buffet Stations

Set up a build-your-own taco station. Large bowls of seasoned beef or chicken, crispy and soft taco shells, shredded cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, and containers of our signature red sauce and sour cream.
