Charlotte's Hidden Oasis

Serving dual comfort food traditions to travelers and locals near the airport since 1998.

Established in 1998

Miguel's Mexican & American Restaurant was founded in 1998 off Business Center Drive. Hidden away near Charlotte Douglas International Airport and adjacent to corporate industrial centers, we quickly grew from a quiet local secret into a bustling airport landmark.

Pilots, flight crews, warehouse workers, and neighborhood regulars all gather here daily to enjoy a relaxing, authentic, and affordable escape.

Two Worlds, One Kitchen

Why choose between Mexican street comfort and classic American dining? Our kitchen was built to honor both. We prepare authentic Mexican specialties, from slow-baked beans and scratch-made red sauce to sizzling grilled fajitas, right alongside seared cheeseburgers and crispy basket chicken tenders.

This dual-culinary focus ensures that every member of the family finds their favorite dish, served hot, fresh, and styled to perfection.

Honest Hospitality

At Miguel's, we have remained committed to our core values: massive portions, affordable pricing, and fast, warm service. We believe in providing an unpretentious space where the community can share a meal, travelers can grab a satisfying bite before a flight, and everyone is welcomed like family.
