Our Story

A neighborhood bakery and brunch spot in the heart of NoDa — made from scratch, run with heart.

Local Loaf started with a simple idea: create a place where the neighborhood could gather over really good food made from scratch. Not fancy. Not fussy. Just honest, delicious food served in a warm, welcoming space.

We bake our bread, pastries, and cookies fresh every morning. Our eggs come from local farms. Our coffee is roasted nearby. And our menu — from shakshuka to shrimp and grits, chicken and waffles to orange chicken — reflects the kind of food we love to eat: comforting, creative, and made with care.

We believe great food brings people together. Whether you're stopping in for a morning coffee and a Snickerdoodle the size of your face, or settling in for dinner and a bottle of wine, Local Loaf is your table.

Our space on East 35th Street is casual and cozy — string lights, friendly faces, and the smell of fresh bread hitting you the moment you walk in. We don't take reservations (just walk in!), and we treat every guest like family. Chef Adam leads our kitchen and hosts seasonal beer dinners that pair thoughtful dishes with local brews.

🍞🍳🥖

We're proud to be part of the NoDa community — a neighborhood that values creativity, connection, and good food. From our early morning bakers to our late-night dishwashers, every member of the Local Loaf team is committed to making your visit special.
