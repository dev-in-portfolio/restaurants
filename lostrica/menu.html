Seasonal Menus

Our menus change with the seasons. Below is a glimpse of the current offerings — exact dishes evolve weekly based on what's freshest from our local farms and purveyors.

Signature Tasting Menu

10+ Courses · $195 per person

A memorable exploration of seasonal ingredients, global techniques, and thoughtful wine pairings. Each course is designed to surprise, delight, and tell a story — from the first amuse to the final mignardise.

Tasting Menu

5–7 Courses · $120 per person

An abbreviated progression of the Signature experience. Perfect for guests seeking a shorter but equally thoughtful journey through the season's bounty.

Reservations Required

Tasting menus require advance booking. Dietary restrictions and allergies can be accommodated with advance notice — please note them when reserving.

Sunday Supper

Reservations required · Single seating at 5:30 PM · $85–90 per person

Each Sunday, we transport our guests to a different corner of the world. Themes change weekly — from Amalfi Coast to Korean Night, Provençal to Carolina Piedmont. A multi-course fixed menu that's part pop-up, part tradition.
