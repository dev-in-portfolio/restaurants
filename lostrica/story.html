Our Story

Where tradition and curiosity meet — a James Beard Award recipient's vision for seasonal, globally inspired dining in Charlotte's Montford neighborhood.

L'Ostrica was born from a simple belief: that the best food tells a story. Chef Eric Ferguson — a James Beard Award recipient — and his partner Cat Carter set out to create a restaurant that celebrates the seasonal gifts of the Carolina Piedmont while embracing global culinary traditions.

L'Ostrica blends tradition and curiosity to create dining experiences that celebrate the best of every season. We design each season's menu through the unique lens of our combined experiences — in partnership with local farms and high-quality purveyors. — Eric & Cat

Eric's career has taken him from Michelin-starred kitchens in New York to the Carolina coast, where he developed a deep respect for locally sourced ingredients and thoughtful technique. Cat brings a curatorial eye and a passion for hospitality that shapes every guest's experience — from the first impression to the last bite.

👨‍🍳 Eric Ferguson 🤝 Cat Carter

Together, they've built a restaurant where the dining room feels like an extension of their home — intimate, approachable, and filled with warmth. The space, located in the Emerson Apartments on Park Road, is designed to make guests feel comfortable enough to linger, yet excited by what appears on the next plate.

Our menu changes with the seasons — sometimes weekly. We draw inspiration from Italian, Korean, Japanese, and French cuisines, but we never let tradition constrain us. Curiosity is our guide. Whether it's a 10-course tasting journey or a relaxed Sunday Supper with a rotating global theme, every meal at L'Ostrica is an exploration.
