Our Story

Bringing the flavors of Italy to Dilworth

Dolce Osteria was born from a love of regional Italian cooking and a desire to create a place where every meal feels like a Sunday dinner in Italy. Founded by Rita and her family, the restaurant opened on Kenilworth Avenue in Charlotte's Dilworth neighborhood with a simple mission: serve authentic, handcrafted Italian food in an intimate, welcoming setting.

The name "Dolce Osteria" translates to "sweet tavern" — a nod to the warm, unpretentious spirit of Italian osterias where good food, good wine, and good company come together naturally. Every dish on our menu reflects time-honored techniques passed down through generations, from the rolling of fresh pasta to the slow simmering of ragu.

Our kitchen is led by a team that believes in the power of simplicity. We import Italian olive oil, source local produce, and make our pasta, sauces, and desserts from scratch daily. The result is a menu that changes with the seasons while staying true to its roots.

Whether you're celebrating an anniversary or simply craving a perfect bowl of pasta, Dolce Osteria is a place where you can slow down, savor the moment, and taste the love that goes into every plate.

Our Journey

2016
A Dream Takes Shape
Rita envisions an authentic Italian osteria in Dilworth — a neighborhood in need of a warm, family-run dinner spot.
2017
Dolce Osteria Opens
The doors open on Kenilworth Avenue with a small dining room, a handmade pasta station, and an ambitious menu.
2019
A Neighborhood Favorite
Regulars fill the dining room nightly. The tiramisu and limoncello cake develop a loyal following.
2021
Expanding the Vision
Lunch service launches Wednesday through Friday. The wine list grows to showcase regional Italian producers.
Today
Still Family-Run
Dolce Osteria continues to serve handcrafted Italian cuisine — made from scratch, served with heart.

What We Stand For

Handmade Always

Regional Roots

Our inspiration comes from Italy's regions — from Piedmont to Sicily — interpreted with respect and creativity.

Seasonal & Local

We partner with Carolina farms for produce and proteins, letting the seasons guide our menu changes.

Warm Hospitality

Every guest is treated like family. We believe great food is only half the experience — the other half is how you're welcomed.
