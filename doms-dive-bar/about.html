About Dom's

South End's neighborhood dive — unfussy, affordable, and yours

The Dive Life

Dom's Dive Bar is exactly what it sounds like — a comfy neighborhood dive bar where the drinks are cheap, the food is craveable, and nobody's pretending to be anything they're not. Tucked into a strip on South Boulevard in Sedgefield, Dom's is the kind of place where you walk in, grab a pool cue, and suddenly it's last call.

With two free pool tables, dart boards, board games, and an impressive TV and sound system, Dom's is built for hanging out. Whether you're coming for Monday's half-off wings, Wednesday's $4 local drafts, or just a Saturday afternoon with $4 Modelos and a Gameday Cheesesteak — there's always a reason to stop by.

"Low-key neighborhood bar vibe. Exactly the kind of bar food you want — no frills, satisfying, and hits the spot."
