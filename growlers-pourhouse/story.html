Our Story

A neighborhood bar serving curated craft food & drinks since 2014

Welcome to Growlers

A lot of different businesses have called this corner spot home. Now it's yours.

Since the mid-century build, this corner at 35th and N. Davidson has been home to Harrell's Food Store, Layton's Sales Company, Villa Heights Furniture, and Aardvark Gallery. The warmth of our 1930s brick walls and the energy of the windows have been a beacon — drawing all types of people from every nook of town to gather in our cozy pub.

Including our staff. We all hang out here. And it isn't just the place we socialize — we've teamed up across our other restaurant concepts to develop a menu that represents a full team collaboration.

“Welcome, we hope you enjoy this place as much as we do.”
— Jeff & Jamie

Timeline

From corner store to neighborhood pub

Mid-Century Harrell's Food Store opens on the corner of 35th and N. Davidson
Later Years Layton's Sales Company, Villa Heights Furniture, and Aardvark Gallery each call this spot home
2014 Jeff Tonidandel (Certified Cicerone) and Jamie Brown open Growlers Pourhouse — a neighborhood bar focused on craft beer and beer food
Today A beloved NoDa institution, part of a family of concepts including Ever Andalo, Haberdish, Olivelli Deli, Leluia Hall, and Supperland
