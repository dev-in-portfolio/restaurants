What we are

Craft beer & beer food — that's it. That's the idea.

What we've got is simple: Craft Beer & Beer Food. All-American craft beer, including selections from our traditional beer engine, paired with craft Americana food — oysters, burgers, sandwiches, fries, and wings in a handful of different flavors. All made in house, every ingredient chosen to taste great with a brew or classic cocktail.

🍺

Beer Engine

Traditional cask-conditioned ale served through a hand pump — a rare find in Charlotte.

🦪

Oysters & Small Plates

Fresh-shucked oysters, shareable plates and bar snacks built for pairing.

🍔

Classic Burger & Wings

House-made burgers, hand-cut fries, and wings in multiple house flavors.

Local Farms

Partnered with Bush & Vine, Boy & Girl Farm, and Fair Share Farm for the freshest ingredients.

Certified Cicerone Owned

Owner Jeff Tonidandel is a Certified Cicerone — think of it as a sommelier for beer. Every pint, every cask, every pairing on our menu is backed by professional-level beer knowledge.

The restaurant is owned by Jeff Tonidandel and his wife Jamie Brown, the team behind Ever Andalo, Haberdish, Olivelli Deli, Leluia Hall, and Supperland.
