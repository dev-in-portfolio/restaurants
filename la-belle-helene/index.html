À Propos

An Authentic Parisian Experience

Step into La Belle Hélène and find yourself transported to a grand Parisian brasserie. The soaring 25-ft ceilings, hand-blown chandeliers, and pewter bar top evoke the timeless elegance of France's finest dining rooms.

Located in the Bank of America Corporate Center at 300 S Tryon Street, our 6,800-square-foot space seats 166 guests across the main dining room, bar, and quaint patio — with operable windows that open to Tryon Street and four private dining rooms for intimate gatherings.

Beneath the direction of Chef Jamie Lynch, a Bravo Top Chef alum, our kitchen honors French culinary tradition while embracing the bounty of the Carolinas.

Chef Jamie Lynch

A Bravo Top Chef alum and celebrated figure in the Charlotte culinary scene, Chef Jamie Lynch brings decades of experience and a deep reverence for French technique to La Belle Hélène.

As Chef Partner of the 5th Street Group, Jamie oversees a culinary program that balances classical French discipline with the seasonal, ingredient-driven ethos of the Carolinas.

"A brasserie should feel both grand and welcoming — like the best meal you've ever had in Paris, but in the heart of Charlotte."
