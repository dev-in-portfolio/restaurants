Our Story

From Srebrenica to Central Avenue.

In 1998, Dino Mehic and his wife Amela came to Charlotte as Bosnian refugees. Dino's father was lost in the Srebrenica massacre three years earlier. They settled in the Central Avenue corridor, where Bosnian refugees were gathering.

In 2003, they opened Bosna Market to serve their community. When they moved to larger quarters, they added a tiny cafe by opening a doorway through the market and stepping down into a small dining room. Euro Grill & Cafe was born.
