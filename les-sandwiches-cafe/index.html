SINCE 2004

The hidden location became part of the legend.

Le's earned its reputation inside Asian Corner Mall with sandwiches that outperformed the surroundings. Because that property has faced redevelopment, a production site must clearly show the restaurant's current active location rather than preserve an obsolete hallway story as directions.

The concept keeps the beloved hidden-gem identity while making the actual current address, parking, hours and pickup instructions impossible to miss.

The current Charlotte location comes first.

Exact address, phone, hours, menu and ordering would be confirmed directly with Le's before launch. No stale Asian Corner Mall directions would remain unless that is still the active location.
