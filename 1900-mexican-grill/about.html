Our Story

A Mexico City cantina, reimagined for Charlotte.

Born in Mexico City.
Built in Charlotte.

Founded in 2009 by Jose G. and Maria De Garcia, 1900 Mexican Grill was born from a love letter to Mexico City — its bustling markets, its neighborhood cantinas, and the warmth of a table shared with family and strangers alike.

What started as a single location on Elizabeth Ave has grown into two Charlotte institutions: a Midtown flagship and a South Park favorite, serving scratch-made Mexican cuisine for over 16 years.

The name "1900" nods to a time when Mexico City was reinventing itself — a city of bold flavors, bold art, and bold identity. That spirit lives on in every plate and every pour.

The Experience

🌮 Scratch Kitchen

Every tortilla, every salsa, every mole — made in-house from scratch. Our kitchen doesn't cut corners because your table doesn't deserve it.

🪅 Lucha Libre

Our walls are lined with an ever-growing collection of lucha libre masks — each one a piece of Mexican wrestling culture. Neon-lit and unapologetically bold, just like the city that inspired us.

🌵 Mezcal Capital of the South

We proudly hold the largest collection of Mezcal in North Carolina — over 80 varieties. From smoky village-pit mezcals to rare artisanal bottles, our mezcal program is second to none.

🍹 25+ Margaritas

Watermelon Jalapeño. Hibiscus. Classic. Seasonal rotations. Our original margaritas are crafted fresh daily — available by the glass or by the pitcher.

🥑 Tableside Guacamole

Guacamole Fresco prepared at your table — choose your heat level, watch it come together, and taste the difference fresh makes.

🎉 Community

1900 is where Charlotte comes together. Good for date nights, group dinners, kids' birthdays, and Tuesday night margaritas with the people you love.
