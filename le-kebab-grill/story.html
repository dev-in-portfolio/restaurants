Our Story

From Jordan to Charlotte — a family tradition of flavor

Our Origin

A Jordanian Culinary Tradition

Le Kebab Grill was born from a simple belief: great food brings people together.

Our story begins in Jordan, where the founder grew up in a kitchen filled with the aromas of sizzling kebabs, fresh herbs, and simmering stews. Family gatherings revolved around shared plates and long meals, where recipes were passed down by hand and heart.

When our family moved to Charlotte, they brought those recipes with them. What started as cooking for friends and community gatherings soon became a dream to share Jordanian hospitality with the wider Charlotte community.

The Chef

Meet the Chef & Owner

Cooking is in his blood. Hospitality is in his nature.

Our chef and owner brings decades of experience from Jordan's kitchens to Charlotte's University City neighborhood. Trained in traditional Mediterranean cooking and inspired by his mother's home kitchen, every dish at Le Kebab Grill reflects his commitment to authenticity.

He personally selects every ingredient, oversees every marinade, and greets every guest who walks through the door. For him, running Le Kebab Grill isn't just about food — it's about building community around a shared table.
