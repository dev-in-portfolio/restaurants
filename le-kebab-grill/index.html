Our Story

Jordanian Flavor, Charlotte Home

Le Kebab Grill brings the authentic taste of Jordan to the University City neighborhood. Our chef and founder uses traditional family recipes passed down through generations, bringing bold Mediterranean flavors to every dish we serve.

From our hand-formed kofta kebabs to our slow-roasted shawarma, every item on our menu is made with care using the freshest ingredients.
