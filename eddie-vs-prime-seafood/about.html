Our Story

About Eddie V's

Seafood, Steaks & Rhythm — a luxury dining experience crafted with passion.

A Destination for
the Senses

Eddie V's Prime Seafood is more than a restaurant — it's a luxury dining destination designed for those who appreciate the finer things. From the moment you arrive, every detail has been considered: the warm glow of elegant lighting, the rich texture of sultry earth tones, and the bold artwork that lines our walls.

Our Charlotte location sits at the historic corner of Trade & Tryon in Uptown — the crossroads of the city, where Charlotte's past and present converge. It's the perfect setting for a restaurant that honors culinary tradition while embracing the rhythm of the modern evening.

01

The Seafood

We source the finest seafood from pristine waters around the globe — cold-water lobster from South Africa, Chilean sea bass from the deep Pacific, and oysters from the best East and West Coast beds. Every piece arrives fresh, flown in daily, and prepared with the reverence it deserves.

02

The Wine

Our award-winning wine program is a cornerstone of the Eddie V's experience. Recognized by Wine Spectator and curated by our Advanced Sommelier, our list of over 300 labels spans the globe — from rare Bordeaux to hidden Willamette Valley gems. Every bottle is selected to elevate your meal.

03

The Rhythm

Nightly jazz trios fill the V Lounge with live music that sets the tone for your evening. It's the rhythm that transforms dinner into an experience — the sound of a piano, the brush of a snare, the warmth of a saxophone weaving through conversation and candlelight.

The Atmosphere

Step inside and you'll find an atmosphere of refined sophistication — sultry earth tones, bold artwork, and the soft murmur of conversation punctuated by live jazz. The dining room exudes warmth and elegance, while the V Lounge offers a more intimate setting where cocktails flow and music takes center stage.

Whether you're seated at the raw bar watching our chefs work their craft, settled into a plush booth for a celebratory dinner, or perched at the lounge with a hand-shaken cocktail, every seat at Eddie V's is the best seat in the house.

In the Heart of Uptown

Our Charlotte location places you at the center of the city's cultural corridor. We're steps from the Blumenthal Performing Arts Center, Bank of America Stadium, the Spectrum Center, the Charlotte Convention Center, and the NASCAR Hall of Fame — making Eddie V's the perfect pre-show dinner, post-game celebration, or convention evening out.

As part of the Eddie V's national family under Darden Restaurants, our Charlotte location carries forward the brand's legacy of excellence while embracing the unique energy and hospitality of the Queen City.
