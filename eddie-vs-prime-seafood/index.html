Welcome to Eddie V's

A Luxury Dining
Destination

Nestled in the heart of Uptown Charlotte at the historic corner of Trade & Tryon, Eddie V's Prime Seafood is an elegant sanctuary where pristine seafood, prime steaks, and live rhythm converge. From the moment you step through our doors, you're enveloped in sultry earth tones, bold artwork, and the warm glow of a truly special evening.

Whether you're celebrating a milestone, closing a deal, or savoring a night out, every detail at Eddie V's is crafted to make the experience unforgettable.

Prime Seafood

Flown in daily from pristine waters worldwide — from delicate Chilean Sea Bass to sweet Jumbo Gulf Shrimp, our seafood is as pristine as the oceans it comes from.

Award-Winning Wine

Our Wine Spectator-recognized list features over 300 labels, personally curated by our Advanced Sommelier to perfectly complement every dish.

Live Music

Jazz trios perform nightly in the V Lounge, setting the tone for an evening of sophistication and rhythm. Sip a hand-shaken cocktail and let the music move you.
