The Wonders of Peruvian Cuisine

Located on Main Street in historic Pineville, Machu Picchu Peruvian Cuisine & Bar is dedicated to serving authentic culinary treasures of the Andes and Pacific Coast. Our kitchen blends indigenous Incan ingredients with Spanish and Chinese influences, crafting mouthwatering ceviches, stir-fries, and classic Peruvian drinks.
