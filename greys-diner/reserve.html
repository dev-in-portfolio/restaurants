No Reservations Needed

Grey's Diner is walk-in only. No reservations, no waitlists for tables. This is a community kitchen — come as you are, grab a seat, and settle in.

We believe the best meals happen when you don't plan them. Walk in, find the seat that calls to you, and let the kitchen take care of the rest.

What to Expect

The 1948 Fodero Car

Our original diner car seating. Counter-style, booths, jukebox accents. First come, first served — ask to sit in the car.

Counter & Indoor Seating

Classic diner counter seating and indoor tables. The heart of the action — watch the kitchen work while you eat.

Outdoor Seating

When the weather's right, grab a seat outside. Plaza Midwood vibes, fresh air, great food.

Best Times to Visit

Weekday Mornings

7am – 10am. The breakfast rush comes and goes. Great time for a quiet seat and a slow coffee.

Weekend Brunch

8am – 12pm. Our busiest time. Expect a wait — but it's worth it. Put your name on the waitlist and explore Plaza Midwood.

Weekday Dinner

5pm – 8pm. The Fodero car at its best. Relaxed, community energy. Great for families and date nights alike.

Fri–Sat Late

8pm – 10pm. The diner shifts to evening mode. Cocktails, burgers, and the kind of night that makes you a regular.

The Waitlist

During peak hours, we use a waitlist system. Put your name in at the host stand and we'll text you when your seat is ready. No app required — just your name and phone number.

While you wait, grab a coffee from Enderly, explore the neighborhood, or sit on the patio. We'll come find you.
