Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about catering with Amélie's

How far in advance should I order?

We recommend at least 72 hours for drop-off orders and 2 weeks for full-service catering. Custom cakes require 7 days' notice.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes! We offer gluten-free, vegetarian, and vegan options across our catering menu. Just let us know when you request your quote.

What is your delivery area?

We deliver throughout the Charlotte metro area. A delivery fee applies based on distance — we'll confirm when you book.

Can I sample items before ordering?

Absolutely. We offer complimentary tastings for events of 50+ guests. Smaller tastings are available for a nominal fee.

Do you offer rentals (linens, serveware)?

We can arrange rentals through our partner vendors for an additional fee. Ask your event coordinator for details.

What is your cancellation policy?

Full refund for cancellations made 7+ days before the event. 50% refund within 3–7 days. Within 72 hours, we credit your account.
