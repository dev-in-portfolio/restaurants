Our place

Louisiana warmth, right where NoDa gathers.

Boudreaux's sits at the meeting point of two strong personalities: the generous, layered cooking traditions of Louisiana and the colorful, independent energy of Charlotte's NoDa neighborhood.

01 · THE FOOD

Built in layers

Louisiana flavor rarely arrives all at once. It starts with time, a careful base and the steady build of spice, smoke and savory depth.

02 · THE ROOM

Easygoing by nature

This is neighborhood dining, not ceremony. Come dressed for the day you had, bring the people you like and let dinner loosen the edges.

03 · THE BLOCK

Made for NoDa

Murals, music, old brick and new ideas all share the same few blocks. Boudreaux's fits the neighborhood by feeling distinct without feeling precious.

A neighborhood night

Dinner can be the plan—or the beginning of one.

Boudreaux's is positioned for the way NoDa evenings actually happen: meet for food, catch a show, wander the block or stay put because the table is too good to leave.
