Montford Legacy

Discover the 25-year history of our cozy tavern, our massive covered deck patio, and our scratch-made pub fare.

Est. 1999 on Montford

Before Montford Drive became one of Charlotte's premier entertainment corridors, Brazwells Pub opened its doors. Over the past 25 years, we have served as the neighborhood's living room. Through expansions and updates, our core mission remains unchanged: serving cold pints, warm smiles, and hearty food.

Whether you are a first-time visitor or a regular who has visited for decades, you will find a warm tavern greeting.

The Covered Sports Patio

Our patio is famous throughout South Charlotte. Fully covered and equipped with misting fans for summer and patio heaters for winter, it features a full outdoor bar, multiple HD screen arrays for game days, pool tables, and comfortable lounge seating for group tailgates.

Comfort Kitchen Standards

We believe classic pub fare deserves scratch-made execution. Our kitchen bread-plates cod fillets for our fish & chips, double-fries our signature waffle fries, and mixes our house buffalo dip daily. No shortcuts—just real, rich comfort food to pair with your beer.
