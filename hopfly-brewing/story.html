Our Story

Two cities, one brewery, and a whole lot of community.

From the Ground Up

HopFly Brewing Company was born from a simple belief: great beer starts with great people. What began as homebrew experiments shared among friends grew into a full-fledged brewery with taprooms in Charlotte and Rocky Mount, North Carolina.

We focus on what we love — hazy IPAs, crisp lagers, and experimental sours — while staying deeply rooted in the communities we serve. Every pint we pour is a reflection of the people, places, and partnerships that make North Carolina special.

Charlotte — The Gold District

In March 2022, we opened our flagship taproom at 1327 S Mint St in Charlotte's historic Gold District, just south of Uptown. The neighborhood — once the center of Charlotte's gold rush — is now a vibrant hub of breweries, restaurants, and artists.

Our Charlotte location features The Loft, a rooftop bar with sweeping skyline views, a dog-friendly patio, rotating food trucks, and a pool table. It's a gathering place for everyone — from after-work crews to weekend adventurers.

The Gold District felt like home from day one. It's a place where history meets reinvention, much like HopFly itself.

Rocky Mount — Eastern NC Roots

Our second taproom at 1151 Falls Rd, Ste. 2035, Rocky Mount brought HopFly to the Twin Counties. Eastern North Carolina has its own rhythm — slower, warmer, and built on community. Our Rocky Mount space reflects that: a welcoming taproom where neighbors become regulars and regulars become family.

We're proud to serve Rocky Mount and the surrounding areas with the same dedication to quality beer and genuine hospitality that defines everything we do.
