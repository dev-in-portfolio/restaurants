Events & Community

There's always something happening at HopFly. From weekly rides to private parties, come be part of it.

HopFly Bike and Brew Cycling Club

Every Tuesday & Thursday | Charlotte Taproom

Grab your bike and join the HopFly crew for a group ride through Charlotte's South End and beyond. All paces welcome — we ride together, then grab a beer together. Routes vary weekly and end back at the taproom.

  • Meet: 1327 S Mint St, Charlotte
  • Roll out: 6:30 PM sharp
  • Distance: 12–18 miles, no-drop
  • Post-ride: First pint discount for riders

Mindless Minutia Trivia

Every Wednesday | Charlotte Taproom

Test your knowledge on obscure facts, pop culture, and the completely useless. Teams up to 6 people. Winner takes home a round of beers and eternal bragging rights.

  • Time: 7:00 PM – 9:00 PM
  • Teams: Up to 6 players
  • Prize: pitcher of your choice

Private Events at The Loft

Charlotte Rooftop | Capacity: 60–80 guests

Our rooftop bar, The Loft, is available for private buyouts and special events. With sweeping skyline views, a dedicated bar, and the full HopFly beer selection, it's the perfect setting for corporate events, birthday parties, rehearsal dinners, and more.

  • Dedicated bartender and HopFly staff
  • Custom beer and cider packages
  • Food truck coordination available
  • AV equipment for presentations
