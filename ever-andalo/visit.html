Reservations

Reservations are available through Tock. A prepaid reservation fee of $3 per person is applied, which goes toward your meal.

Walk-ins are always welcome. If we have availability, we'll seat you — no reservation needed.

For parties of 8 or more, please call us directly at (704) 910-6543.

Parking

Street parking is available along N Davidson St and surrounding NoDa side streets. The neighborhood can fill up on weekend evenings — we recommend arriving a few minutes early or using a rideshare.

The NoDa Neighborhood

NoDa (North Davidson) is Charlotte's arts and entertainment district — a walkable neighborhood of galleries, live music venues, coffee shops, and some of the city's best restaurants. Ever Andalo sits right in the middle of it all.
