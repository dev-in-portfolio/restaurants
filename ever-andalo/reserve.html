What to Expect

A cozy evening in NoDa — Italian food, Italian wine, and good company.

Reservations via Tock

Book your table online through Tock. A prepaid fee of $3 per person secures your reservation and goes toward your meal.

Walk-Ins Welcome

Don't have a reservation? Walk-ins are always welcome. If we have a table available, we'll seat you. You can also join our waitlist in person.

Large Parties

Bringing a group of 8 or more? Please call us at (704) 910-6543 to arrange your reservation. We'll make sure your group is taken care of.

The Experience

Start with burrata and focaccia. Share a pasta or two. Let our sommeliers guide you through the wine list. End with amaro. That's an evening at Ever Andalo.

The Neighborhood

NoDa is one of Charlotte's most walkable neighborhoods. Before or after dinner, explore the galleries, coffee shops, and boutiques along N Davidson Street.
