Getting Here

Right off I-77 Exit 3.

Cafe South is located at the intersection of West Arrowood Road and Forest Pine Drive in South Charlotte. Take I-77 Exit 3 and head toward the intersection. You'll find the cafe near the Montclaire South, Old Whitehall, and Ayrsley neighborhoods.

The restaurant is around the corner from Ramblewood Park and Central Piedmont Community College's Harper Campus. If you're heading to Carowinds or the North Carolina Welcome Center, Cafe South is a natural stop just before or after the state line.

Nearby landmarks: Ramblewood Park, CPCC Harper Campus, NC Welcome Center, Carowinds
