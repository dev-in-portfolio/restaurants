Our Story

The Beginning

Built on a simple idea.

Cafe South was founded with the vision of creating a warm, inviting space where guests could enjoy hearty meals reminiscent of home cooking. The goal was personal: make a place where regulars feel known, visitors feel welcome, and every plate tastes like it was made for family.

That idea hasn't changed. Since 2012, the cafe has served South Charlotte with a menu rooted in real cooking — farm-fresh eggs any style, scratch-made biscuits, chicken and waffles, country fried steak, grilled seafood, and daily specials that rotate with the week.

The Neighborhood

Where South Charlotte meets.

Cafe South sits at the intersection of West Arrowood Road and Forest Pine Drive, right off I-77 Exit 3. The location connects the Montclaire South, Old Whitehall, and Ayrsley neighborhoods — and sits around the corner from Ramblewood Park and Central Piedmont Community College's Harper Campus.

For travelers heading to or from Carowinds, the North Carolina Welcome Center, or the I-77 corridor, the cafe has become a reliable stop: a place to eat well without the highway strip feeling.

What we stand for

The kitchen runs on real cooking.

🍲

Home-Cooked, Not Assembly

Every egg is cracked to order. Biscuits are made fresh. Gravy is white country gravy, not a shortcut. The kitchen cooks like a home kitchen that happens to seat 60.

🎁

Daily Specials, Real Rotation

The specials board changes throughout the week: beef tips, spaghetti, baked chicken, grilled pork chops, and more. Call ahead to ask what's on today.

👥

Community First

Cafe South participates in local events, supports nearby businesses, and collaborates with neighborhood organizations. The regulars know the staff by name.

Health & Quality

A 96 out of 100 health score.

Cafe South maintains a strong health department rating, reflecting the kitchen's commitment to cleanliness, food safety, and preparation standards. Every plate that leaves the pass is held to the same standard the owners would want at their own table.
