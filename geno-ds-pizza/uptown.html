The Market at 7th Street

A food hall experience in the center of Uptown

Geno D's Pizza is proudly located inside The Market at 7th Street — Uptown Charlotte's premier food hall at the corner of 7th and Brevard. Just steps from the Charlotte Hornets' Spectrum Center, this historic building brings together a curated collection of local food vendors under one roof.

You can smell Geno D's the second you hit the door. Follow your nose to our corner of the market, where Geno D and Gena are serving up hot slices, Grandma pies, garlic knots, and cannoli fresh daily.
