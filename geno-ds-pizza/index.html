Welcome to Geno D's

Bringing Jersey Shore pizza straight to Charlotte

You can smell Geno D's the second you hit the door in The Market at 7th Street. Family-owned and operated, we bring Jersey Shore style pizza straight to Charlotte — crafted from 35 years of perfected "Old World" family recipes. Our version of New York style dough, topped with quality ingredients and served up fast by Geno D and his daughter Gena, makes this your go-to pizzeria Uptown.

🍕

Grandma Pizza

Thin Sicilian crust, fresh mozzarella, strips of sauce, and a special pesto finish — our signature.

🍯

Hot Honey Pizza
