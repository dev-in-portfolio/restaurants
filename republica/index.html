The Spirit of the Caribbean

Located on East Independence Boulevard, República Restaurant & Lounge brings Dominican gourmet dining to Matthews. We hand-craft traditional staples like Sancocho stews, crispy pork Mofonguitos, and Chimi burgers with a high-end culinary touch, transforming into a vibrant lounge with live music and tropical cocktails on weekends.
