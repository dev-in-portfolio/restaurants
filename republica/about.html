Dominican Culinary Roots

Founded in Matthews, República Restaurant & Lounge is dedicated to serving authentic gourmet Dominican recipes. Our kitchen blends indigenous Taíno, Spanish, and African influences, crafting mouthwatering Mofongos, braised chicken platters, and traditional Sancocho stews.

Whether celebrating over a family Sunday dinner or sipping cocktails on our patio during Latin nights, we present the hospitality of the islands.

January 2026 Grand Re-Opening

Following a temporary update in late 2025, we celebrated our Grand Re-opening in January 2026, presenting a fully upgraded gourmet wok kitchen, expanded lounge seating, and an interactive cocktail bar. We invite you to experience the rhythms and recipes of Santo Domingo.
