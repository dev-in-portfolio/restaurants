Our Story

Over 30 years of brick oven pizza, family hospitality, and Montford neighborhood tradition.

Luisa's Brick Oven Pizzeria has been a Charlotte institution since 1991. For over three decades, we've been serving the Montford and Madison Park neighborhoods the same way: fresh dough stretched by hand, quality ingredients, and a blazing hot brick oven that gives every pizza its signature crispy-yet-chewy crust.

Owner Jeff has been the heart of Luisa's for years — greeting regulars by name, making sure every pizza comes out right, and creating a warm, family-friendly atmosphere that keeps generations of Charlotteans coming back. The same faces have been tossing dough and sliding pizzas into that brick oven for years, and that consistency is something we're proud of.

"Luisa's is a humble, cozy, sweet place to come and eat with your family. The big stone oven is right there for you to see the pizzas being baked. People baking pizza have been working there forever — same guys! The owner Jeff is the heart of the place, so welcoming."
1991
Luisa's opens on Abbey Place — a neighborhood pizzeria with a brick oven and big dreams
1994
Already a local favorite, known for generous toppings and that perfect brick oven crust
2000s
Luisa's becomes a Montford institution — families, sports teams, and date nights alike
Today
Still family-owned, still serving the same great pizza — and still using that same brick oven

Our brick oven is the centerpiece of the restaurant — visible from the dining room, always roaring hot, and tended by the same skilled hands that have been making Luisa's pizza for years. We use only the freshest ingredients and the finest cheeses. Our dough is made from scratch daily. Our sauce is a family recipe. And our pizzas are loaded with toppings — no skimping here.
