Our Story

Thai authenticity and sushi craftsmanship in Charlotte since 2009

Bringing Thai Flavors to Charlotte

Deejai Thai has been a Charlotte favorite for over 17 years, serving authentic Thai cuisine and fresh sushi from its Providence Road location in Myers Park. Built on a foundation of traditional family recipes and the highest-quality ingredients, Deejai brings the bold, balanced flavors of Thailand to the Queen City.

The name "Deejai" means "good heart" in Thai — and that philosophy runs through everything we do. From the careful preparation of each curry paste to the artful rolling of every sushi maki, our team takes pride in creating food that nourishes both body and spirit. We believe in spice that challenges, flavors that surprise, and service that makes you feel like family.

Our Philosophy

Authentic Thai Traditions

Every dish starts with traditional Thai techniques — hand-pounded curry pastes, fresh herbs, and the perfect balance of sweet, sour, salty, and spicy.

Fresh Sushi, Daily

Our sushi bar sources the freshest fish and prepares each roll to order — from classic nigiri to our signature Deejai Roll.

Spice Is Personal

We believe heat should be your choice — from mild and aromatic to Thai hot. Our spice finder helps you find your perfect level.

Community First

For over 17 years, Deejai has been part of the Myers Park and Charlotte community — serving families, neighbors, and visitors alike.
