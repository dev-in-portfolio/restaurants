Draft Selection

Year-Round Flagships

Jalapeño Pale AleFavorite 5.5% ABV

Our signature unfiltered pale ale brewed with fresh local jalapeños. Bright pepper aroma with a clean, smooth finish and none of the heat.

Higher Ground IPA 7.0% ABV

West Coast style IPA dry-hopped with plenty of Cascade hops. Boasting robust pine, citrus peel aromas, and a balanced bitter finish.

Rewind Lager 4.4% ABV

A highly crushable, traditional German-style pilsner. Clean, crisp, light-bodied, and perfect for warm patio gatherings.

Seasonal & Limited Taps

Sunspree Hazy IPA 6.8% ABV

A juicy, double dry-hopped hazy IPA loaded with Citra and Mosaic hops. Boasting bold notes of mango, passionfruit, and sweet grapefruit.

Lazy Bird Brown Ale 5.5% ABV

A classic American brown ale brewed with roasted chocolate malts, giving notes of hazelnut, cocoa, and a clean, dry finish.

Paradise Session IPA 4.8% ABV

Light and sessionable tropical IPA dry-hopped with Citra, offering notes of fresh pine needles and orange zest.
