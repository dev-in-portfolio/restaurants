Our Green Brewhouse Initiatives

Energy & Water

Rooftop Solar Array

Our 220 rooftop solar panels generate approximately 75,000 kWh of clean electricity annually, offsetting 100% of our brewhouse requirements during peak hours.

Water Reclamation Program

We use custom heat exchangers to capture and reclaim hot water from the brewing process, recycling it directly for subsequent washes and batches.

Agriculture & Waste

Spent Grain Composting

100% of our spent brewing grains (barley, wheat, oats) are donated to local Charlotte farms. This feeds livestock and fertilizes soils instead of entering landfills.

Eco-Friendly PakTechs

We package our 6-packs using only 100% recycled plastic PakTech carriers. Bring them back to the taproom to be recycled again in our closed-loop bin.
