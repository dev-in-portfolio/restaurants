Fresh Beer, Solar-Powered Community

Welcome to Birdsong Brewing Co., one of Charlotte's original craft taprooms on the border of NoDa. Since 2011, we have focused on brewing sessionable, balanced beers for the community. With 20 active taps, free roasted peanuts, a dog-friendly outdoor deck, and a 100% solar-powered brewhouse, we provide a casual, welcoming space to gather.
