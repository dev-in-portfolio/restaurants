Brewed Under the Sun

Learn about our commitment to eco-conscious craft brewing and local community partnerships on Davidson Street.

100% Solar-Powered Brewhouse

In our pursuit of sustainable operations, Birdsong installed a comprehensive solar array on the roof of our Davidson Street taproom. Our 80+ solar panels capture Carolina sunshine to power our entire brewhouse, keeping our footprint light and our hops fresh.

By using clean energy to brew, we prevent tons of greenhouse gas emissions annually, proving that local craft beer can support both a great flavor and a green planet.

Supporting Local Cattle Farms

Brewing produces substantial spent grain mash. Instead of sending this organic material to local landfills, Birdsong donates 100% of our spent brewing grains to family-owned cattle farms in Piedmont, NC.

Local cows enjoy a nutritious, sweet malt feed, and our brewery achieves a closed-loop recycling cycle that keeps waste at an absolute zero.

Community, Art & Free Peanuts

Since opening in 2011, Birdsong has remained deeply rooted in Charlotte's neighborhood culture. We display murals by local NoDa artists in our taproom, host regular running clubs, and donate $1 of selected pints to local nonprofits. Plus, our signature free roasted peanuts are always warm and ready for shell-cracking at the bar!
