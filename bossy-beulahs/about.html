Beulah's Legacy

Discover the heritage behind our recipes, our hormone-free chicken pledge, and our local baking partnerships.

Who Was Beulah?

Beulah was the great-aunt of our founder, Jim Noble. She spent her life cooking for family and friends in North Carolina, specializing in crisp fried chicken. She was notoriously "bossy" in the kitchen, demanding absolute precision with fryer temperatures, batter thickness, and seasoning ratios.

Today, we honor her name and high standards by preparing every chicken breast exactly as she would have.

Our Pasture-Raised Pledge

We believe premium flavor starts with premium farming. Bossy Beulah's serves only all-natural chicken raised on pastures, free of hormones, antibiotics, and artificial additives. This high-quality sourcing ensures our chicken remains tender, juicy, and naturally flavorful.

Local Bakery Partnerships

A great sandwich requires a great bun. We partner with Charlotte's own Copain Bakery to bake and deliver fresh, soft brioche buns and cookies daily. Supporting local bakers allows us to guarantee fresh, artisan buns for every customer.
