The Legend of The Beaut

At Bossy Beulah's, we believe in doing one thing beautifully: the perfect fried chicken sandwich. Named after our founder's grandmother Beulah, who spent decades perfecting her crisp pressure-fry seasoning, our signature sandwich "The Beaut" features a local bakery brioche bun, house-cured pickles, Duke's mayonnaise, and premium pasture-raised chicken.
