Our Story

Three generations of burgers in Monroe, NC

Since 1951

Duke's Grill has been a Monroe institution since the mid-20th century, serving fresh ground chuck burgers to generations of Union County families. What started as a simple burger stand grew into a beloved local landmark where neighbors gather over hand-patted patties, giant onion rings, and sweet tea.

The restaurant is known for its Slaw Burger — a signature creation that was voted the best burger in North Carolina. The "All the Way" order, topped with onion rings and homemade ranch, is a rite of passage for first-timers.

1970s–1990s

Named best burger in North Carolina by statewide poll

Still family-run, still fresh-ground, still Monroe's favorite
