Our Story

A passion for authentic Italian cuisine

Meet the Chef

Chef Fernando Campoverde

Born and raised in Ecuador with deep Italian roots, Chef Fernando Campoverde discovered his love for Italian cuisine at a young age, spending summers in the kitchens of his Italian grandparents. After culinary training at the International Culinary Center in New York and apprenticing in some of Tuscany's most celebrated trattorias, Fernando brought his passion and expertise to Charlotte.

In 2008, he opened Fiamma Ristorante in the heart of Dilworth with a simple mission: to create an authentic Italian dining experience where every dish tells a story. His philosophy is rooted in tradition — using time-honored techniques, the finest imported Italian ingredients, and locally sourced produce from North Carolina farms.

Every pasta at Fiamma is handmade daily. Every sauce is crafted from scratch. Every meal is an invitation to slow down and savor the moment, just as they do in Italy.

Zagat's Highest Rated Italian in Charlotte

Since 2008, Fiamma Ristorante has been recognized by Zagat as the highest-rated Italian restaurant in Charlotte — a distinction we've proudly maintained through an unwavering commitment to quality, hospitality, and authenticity.

This recognition reflects not just the food we serve, but the experience we create: a warm, welcoming space where every guest feels like family, and every meal becomes a cherished memory.
