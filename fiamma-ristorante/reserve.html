Reservation Details

  • Party Size: We accommodate parties of 1–8 guests for standard reservations. For larger parties, please contact us directly.
  • Cancellation Policy: We kindly ask for 24 hours' notice for cancellations or changes to your reservation.
  • Lateness: Tables are held for 15 minutes past the reservation time. After that, we may need to accommodate other guests.
  • Special Requests: Let us know about dietary restrictions, celebrations, or seating preferences when booking.

Walk-Ins Welcome

We always reserve a portion of our dining room for walk-in guests. Availability varies by night — Tuesday through Thursday typically has the best walk-in availability, while Friday and Saturday are busier.

If you're in the neighborhood and craving Italian, stop by! We'll do our best to seat you as quickly as possible.

Private Parties & Special Events

Planning a special celebration, corporate dinner, or intimate gathering? Fiamma offers private and semi-private dining options to make your event memorable.

Private Dining

Our private dining room accommodates up to 20 guests for an exclusive Italian dining experience with a customized menu.

Semi-Private Events

For parties of 10–30, we can create a dedicated space within our main dining room with tailored menus and décor.

Custom Menus

Work directly with Chef Fernando to design a bespoke menu featuring his signature dishes and seasonal specials.

To inquire about private dining or event bookings, please call us or email info@fiammatoristorante.com.
