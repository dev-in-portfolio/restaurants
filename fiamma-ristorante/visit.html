About Dilworth

Dilworth is one of Charlotte's most charming and historic neighborhoods, known for its tree-lined streets, beautiful Victorian homes, and vibrant local dining scene. Located just minutes from Uptown Charlotte, Dilworth offers a welcoming, walkable community that perfectly complements the Fiamma experience.

Whether you're exploring Dilworth Park, enjoying a stroll along the neighborhood's historic streets, or joining us for a special celebration, Fiamma Ristorante is proud to call Dilworth home.
