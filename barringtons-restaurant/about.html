Heritage & Craft

Celebrating over 25 years of cozy candlelit dining and seasonal New American cuisine in Charlotte.

Chef Bruce Moffett's Vision

"When I opened Barrington’s in 2000, my goal was simple: to create a small, neighborhood restaurant that served the highest level of food with the intimacy of a private home dinner."

After training at the Culinary Institute of America and working in top-tier kitchens in Boston, Chef Bruce Moffett relocated to Charlotte, NC, bringing his love for New England coastal cooking and farm-to-table ethics. Barrington’s was the foundational milestone that led to the formation of the Moffett Restaurant Group.

The Intimate 45-Seat Dining Room

Unlike sprawling modern dining spaces, Barrington's intentionally retains its cozy 45-seat dining footprint in Foxcroft East. This intimate scale means our kitchen staff is close to our tables, our service team knows every repeat guest by name, and the ambient energy feels warm and exclusive.

By avoiding large corporate layouts, we ensure every plate of lobster gnocchi or seared halibut receives the meticulous attention it deserves.

Local Sourcing & Seasonality

We believe that premium cooking requires premium partnerships. We coordinate daily with family-owned farms across North and South Carolina to source organic vegetables, herbs, and dairy. Our seafood is line-caught and flown in daily from coastal docks, allowing us to publish a seasonal menu that shifts fluidly with the region's agricultural cycles.
