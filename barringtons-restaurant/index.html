An Intimate Fine-Dining Experience

Established in 2000, Barrington’s is the restaurant that started it all for Chef Bruce Moffett and the Moffett Restaurant Group. Tucked away in the Foxcroft East Shopping Center in South Charlotte, our cozy, candlelight dining room offers a warm, personal atmosphere where every table is treated like family.
