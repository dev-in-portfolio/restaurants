Asian Street Comfort Food

Welcome to Bao and Broth in Charlotte, NC. Located inside the historic Optimist Hall food hall, we specialize in authentic, vibrant Asian street comfort food. Crafted by award-winning Chef Larry Schreiber, our menu brings together fluffy steamed bao buns, 24-hour slow-cooked ramen broths, and hand-folded potstickers.
