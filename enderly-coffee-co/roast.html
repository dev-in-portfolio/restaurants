Our Sourcing Philosophy

Direct Trade over Fair Trade. Farmer relationships over transactions.

Tony Santoro, our founder and head roaster, purchases beans directly from farmers, paying above-market premiums and building year-over-year relationships. This is Direct Trade—a step beyond Fair Trade certification.

We work with Balzac Brothers, a family-run import house established in 1917, to bring green beans from origin to our roastery. Our SCA-certified roaster ensures every batch is crafted to highlight the unique character of each origin.
