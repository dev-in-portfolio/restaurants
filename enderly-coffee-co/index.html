Our Story

Two teachers who fell in love over coffee.

Becky and Tony Santoro were teachers who shared a love of coffee. What started as a garage roasting hobby grew into one of Charlotte's most beloved coffee brands. In 2012, they opened Enderly Coffee Co. in the Enderly Park neighborhood on Tuckaseegee Road.

Today, Enderly is both a neighborhood cafe and a craft roastery. Tony sources beans directly from farmers, paying above-market premiums and building year-over-year relationships. Every bag is roasted in-house. Every cup is made with care.
