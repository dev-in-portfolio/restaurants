Our Story

Craft beer, community, and the trackside spirit

How It Started

Kit's Trackside Crafts was born from a simple belief: great beer brings people together. Nestled in historic downtown Pineville — just steps from the railroad tracks that give the shop its name — we opened our doors to create a space where craft beer enthusiasts and newcomers alike could explore, discover, and connect.

📸 Historic downtown Pineville — the view from our front door

What Sets Us Apart

With 24 rotating taps and a bottle shop boasting over 350 beers, we offer one of the most thoughtfully curated selections in the Charlotte metro area. But what really sets us apart is expertise. Our staff includes a Certified Cicerone — a beer professional who has passed a rigorous certification program covering beer styles, brewing processes, ingredients, flavors, and food pairing. Whether you're a hop head, a stout lover, or just starting your beer journey, our team can guide you to the perfect pour.

More Than Beer

We believe everyone deserves a great drink. That's why we stock a rotating selection of fine wines, traditional meads, and hard ciders alongside our extensive beer collection. From a crisp Saison on a summer afternoon to a barrel-aged Barleywine by the fire, there's always something new to discover. Our mix & match 4-packs make it easy to explore — grab a flight of bottles to go.

Pineville Proud

We're proud to call downtown Pineville home. From the historic railroad that runs through town to the vibrant Main Street businesses that make this community special, Pineville is where we live, work, and pour. We partner with local organizations, sponsor community events, and believe that a great craft beer shop can be a cornerstone of a great neighborhood.
