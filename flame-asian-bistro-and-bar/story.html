Our Story

Fresh ingredients. Authentic flavor. Modern fire.

Flame: Asian Bistro & Bar was born from a simple belief: that the best Asian food honors tradition while embracing innovation. Located in Pineville, just south of Charlotte, Flame brings together the culinary traditions of China, Japan, Korea and Thailand under one roof — with a modern edge that keeps guests coming back.

The name "Flame" reflects our cooking philosophy: high-heat wok cooking, fire-grilled meats and the bold, vibrant energy of Asian street food culture. The Chinese character 火 (huǒ), meaning "fire," is central to our identity — it represents the heat that gives our dishes their distinctive wok hei, the sear on our Seoul Fire Ribeye, and the warmth of our hospitality.

"Our team shares a passion for serving Asian fusion dishes that blend tradition and innovation. As an Asian restaurant, we're dedicated to creating fresh recipes and delivering personal service. Each team member plays a key role in making your visit special. Our goal is simple — serve food that brings people together and keeps guests coming back."

Our menu is a journey through Asia's most beloved flavor profiles. Start with handmade dim sum — soup dumplings, crystal shrimp har gow, and black golden lava custard buns crafted fresh daily. Move on to shareable appetizers like our signature crab and brie wontons or crispy roasted pork belly. For the main event, choose from wok-fired noodles, Korean-inspired ribeye, Sichuan classics, or Thai curries. Every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients, authentic techniques and a touch of modern creativity.

Behind the bar, our cocktail program brings the same creativity to glassware — lychee-infused martinis, Thai basil smashes, and spicy mules that complement the heat and depth of our food. Weekend mojito specials and a curated selection of beer and wine round out the experience.

Whether you're joining us for a quick lunch, a date-night dinner, a celebration with friends or a catered event, Flame is a place where tradition meets fire — and every meal is an experience.
