FAQs

Do I need a membership to enter?

Yes. A $10/year membership is required for all guests. Members can sign up online or at the door (subject to availability).

How many guests can I bring?

Each member may bring up to three guests per visit. Guests must comply with all house rules and dress code.

Do you take reservations?

Yes — reservations are highly recommended on Fridays and Saturdays. Book through Resy or call (704) 817-3710.

Is there a dress code?

Yes — upscale casual. No athletic wear, gym shoes, or excessively casual attire. When in doubt, dress up.

Can I host a private event?

Absolutely. We offer private buyouts and semi-private event space. Visit our Events page for details.

What's the parking situation?

Free parking is available in the Park Road Shopping Center rear lot. We're located behind the main strip beside Blackhawk Hardware.
